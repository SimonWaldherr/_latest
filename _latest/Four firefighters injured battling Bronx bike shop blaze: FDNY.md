<div><p>Four firefighters were hurt as they battled a massive blaze in a Bronx bike shop overnight, officials said.</p>
<p>The fire broke out inside Arrow Cycle on White Plains Road near East 228th Street in Wakefield around 8:20 p.m. and grew to four alarms, the FDNY said.</p>
<p>Thirty-nine units, including 168 firefighters, responded to the blaze &#8212; which burned for more than four hours.</p>
<p>Three firefighters were taken to local hospitals and one was treated on the scene &#8212; each of them with minor injuries, officials said.</p>
<p>The cause of the fire is under investigation.</p>
