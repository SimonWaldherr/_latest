<div><p>A tsunami warning was issued Monday afternoon after a powerful earthquake struck off the coast of Alaska, according to the National Weather Service.</p>
<p>The 7.4 magnitude quake shook the earth about 55 miles southeast of Sand Point, an island off the southwestern mainland of the state, <a href="https://tsunami.gov/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">according to NOAA</a>.</p>
<p>The quake hit just before 5 p.m. New York time, the agency reported. A tsunami warning was issued for the coast after the strong earthquake.</p>
