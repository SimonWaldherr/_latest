<div><p>The transcript of a deposition given by&#160;Ghislaine Maxwell&#160;during a defamation case by Jeffrey Epstein victim Virginia Roberts Giuffre&#160;has been released to the public.&#160;</p>
<p>Judges rejected Maxwell's arguments to keep the sworn testimony, which contains intimate details of her personal life, under wraps on Wednesday.&#160;</p>
<p>A federal appeals court upheld a decision from a lower court that the deposition given by the 58-year-old socialite and associate of disgraced financier Epstein should be made public.</p>
<p>Maxwell is currently being held at a Brooklyn prison awaiting trial on charges that she helped Epstein recruit underage girls as young as 14 to engage in sexual acts in the mid-1990s.</p>
<p>Ms Giuffre alleges Epstein forced her to have sex with Prince Andrew and others when she was 17. The Duke of York has denied the allegations.</p>
<p>Appealing to the court to keep the documents sealed Maxwell, the daughter of media magnate and former Labour MP&#160;Robert Maxwell, said that publishing the testimony would jeopardise her own <a href="https://www.telegraph.co.uk/news/2020/10/19/maxwell-loses-bid-keep-sworn-testimony-wraps/">defence against the criminal charges she is facing.</a></p>
