<div><p>Click to email this to a friend (Opens in new window)</p><p>Click to share on Twitter (Opens in new window)</p><p>Click to share on Facebook (Opens in new window)</p><p>Bill Cosby is doing better behind bars than his creepy new mugshot might suggest, his team said.</p>
<p>The convicted sex offender was seen smiling and using a telephone on a virtual call with his publicist, Andrew Wyatt, in a new image released after the mugshot <a href="https://nypost.com/2020/10/20/bill-cosby-smiles-in-creepy-new-mug-shot-from-pa-prison/">showed him looking disheveled</a> in the Collegeville, Pa., prison.</p>
<p>&#8220;We are posting this to reassure his family and supporters that he is doing ok during this pandemic,&#8221; his reps <a href="https://twitter.com/BillCosby/status/1318713665248481288" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">wrote Tuesday on his Twitter</a>.</p>
<p>Prison officials recently updated Cosby&#8217;s mug shot, which is done routinely as inmates age at the maximum-security State Correctional Institution.</p>
<p>In the mug shot taken Sept. 4, the 83-year-old former funnyman flashed an eerie smile and had unruly white hair.</p>
<p>The snap instantly became a trending topic online &#8212; sparking debate after some expressed shock and worry over his unkempt appearance.</p>
<p>&#8220;Bill Cosby is trending because a new mug shot has some people sympathizing that he&#8217;s an older man in prison,&#8221; <a href="https://twitter.com/YasmeenTV/status/1318606960984469505" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">wrote one Twitter user</a>.</p>
<p>&#8220;Just to remind you, he was found guilty of three counts of aggravated indecent assault, for drugging and sexually assaulting Andrea Constand at his home.&#8221;</p>
 
<p>Cosby, who was once known as &#8220;America&#8217;s Dad,&#8221; is serving a three- to 10-year prison term on the felony sexual assault charges.</p>
