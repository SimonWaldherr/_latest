cholangitis is a rare liver condition which affects the bile ducts and&#160;gallbladder.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">Bile ducts inside and outside the liver become progressively smaller due to inflammation and scarring of the ducts, though the cause is unknown.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">The condition often occurs alongside other intestinal conditions including IBS and&#160;ulcerative colitis, and there is no specific treatment.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">Symptoms can be mild and include itching and abdominal discomfort, but may become severe and include jaundice and fever, shaking or chills.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">The disease can lead to&#160;cirrhosis of the liver and liver failure, while also increasing the likelihood of various liver cancers.</p><p
