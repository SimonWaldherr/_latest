<p><a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/category/netflix">Netflix</a> is unquestionably the most popular video streaming service, with <a href="https://www.businessinsider.com.au/netflix-q4-earnings-analysis-subscribers-revenue-eps-2019-1">139 million global subscribers</a> watching about 140 million hours of content each day.</p>
<p>You can certainly do that on your smartphone or tablet &#8211; the <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/netflix/id363590051?mt=8">Netflix app for iOS</a> and the <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.netflix.mediaclient&amp;amp;hl=en_US">Netflix app for Android</a> are handy ways to watch.</p>
<p>But what if you prefer to watch <a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/category/netflix">Netflix</a> on a bigger screen, like the TV in your living room? There are five popular ways you could do that.</p>

<h2>Watch Netflix on a smart TV</h2>
<a href="https://static.businessinsider.com/image/57e5be4db0ef9764008b7f80.jpg" rel="modal"><img src="https://static.businessinsider.com/image/57e5be4db0ef9764008b7f80-1200.jpg" class="size_primary" alt=""></a><p class="image_source">Samsung TV/YouTube</p>Most any smart TV will come with Netflix (and many other streaming services) built-in.<p>These days, many large flat screen televisions double as &#8220;smart TVs&#8221; &#8211; they come with a built-in media player that lets you connect to popular video services like Netflix, <a href="https://track.flexlinkspro.com/a.ashx?foid=1067927.140569223&amp;amp;foc=1&amp;amp;fot=9999&amp;amp;fos=1&amp;amp;fobs=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv&amp;amp;url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.hulu.com%2Fwelcome%3Fv%3Da">Hulu</a>, <a href="https://www.amazon.com/tryprimefree?ref_=assoc_tag_ph_1427739975520&amp;amp;_encoding=UTF8&amp;amp;camp=1789&amp;amp;creative=9325&amp;amp;linkCode=pf4&amp;amp;tag=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv-20&amp;amp;linkId=98df2f61b0bf142ad80189213c4ac740">Amazon Prime</a>, and many others via on-screen apps.</p>
<p>If you have a smart TV, all you need to do is subscribe to Netflix and log in via the app on your TV.</p>

<h2>Watch Netflix using a streaming media player like Roku</h2>
<a href="https://static.businessinsider.com/image/580f68fdb28a641b008b48e1.jpg" rel="modal"><img src="https://static.businessinsider.com/image/580f68fdb28a641b008b48e1-1200.jpg" class="size_primary" alt=""></a><p class="image_source">Roku</p>Streaming media players like the Roku Streaming Stick turn any TV into a smart TV.<p>You don&#8217;t need a smart TV, because you can turn any television that has an HDMI input into the equivalent of a smart TV using a media streaming device.</p>
<p>There are a lot of media streamers available, including a family of products from <a href="https://track.flexlinkspro.com/a.ashx?foid=1067927.2905429&amp;amp;foc=2&amp;amp;fot=9999&amp;amp;fos=1&amp;amp;fobs=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv&amp;amp;url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.roku.com%2Fproducts%2Fplayers">Roku</a>, as well as <a href="https://www.amazon.com/b/?ie=UTF8&amp;amp;node=8521791011&amp;amp;tag=googhydr-20&amp;amp;hvadid=317852412431&amp;amp;hvpos=1t1&amp;amp;hvnetw=g&amp;amp;hvrand=11239336086549880693&amp;amp;hvpone=&amp;amp;hvptwo=&amp;amp;hvqmt=b&amp;amp;hvdev=c&amp;amp;hvdvcmdl=&amp;amp;hvlocint=&amp;amp;hvlocphy=9067609&amp;amp;hvtargid=kwd-295678595759&amp;amp;ref=pd_sl_89u52mi2tr_e&amp;amp;_encoding=UTF8&amp;amp;tag=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv-20&amp;amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;amp;linkId=a351667a2073430c340ebc43ac8e201c&amp;amp;camp=1789&amp;amp;creative=9325">Amazon&#8217;s Fire TV</a>, and <a href="https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B01N1NT9Y6/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;amp;tag=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv-20&amp;amp;camp=1789&amp;amp;creative=9325&amp;amp;linkCode=as2&amp;amp;creativeASIN=B01N1NT9Y6&amp;amp;linkId=913d932884be67ef851ac92fd1b6bb72">Nvidia Shield TV</a>.</p>

<h2>Watch Netflix on a gaming console like PS4</h2>
<a href="https://static.businessinsider.com/image/5cf5375b11e205092521a4a5.jpg" rel="modal"><img src="https://static.businessinsider.com/image/5cf5375b11e205092521a4a5-1200.jpg" class="size_primary" alt=""></a><p class="image_source">Sony/Netflix</p>You can use the Netflix app on your PS4 or Xbox One.<p>Game consoles like the <a href="https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07BZNX2DT/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;amp;tag=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv-20&amp;amp;camp=1789&amp;amp;creative=9325&amp;amp;linkCode=as2&amp;amp;creativeASIN=B07BZNX2DT&amp;amp;linkId=c65c9b00bf666ea810ab606605f7f568">PlayStation 4</a> and <a href="http://linksynergy.walmart.com/deeplink?id=EHFxW6yx8Uo&amp;amp;mid=2149&amp;amp;u1=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv&amp;amp;murl=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.walmart.com%2Fip%2FMicrosoft-Xbox-One-X-1TB-Console-Black-CYV-00001%2F276629190%3Fu1%3Dwtbs_5cf5475b1534db0f238d93a3%26oid%3D223073.1%26wmlspartner%3Djo%2FpTdthTuc%26sourceid%3D00851888772926760384%26affillinktype%3D10%26veh%3Daff">Xbox One</a> have their own suite of streaming video apps &#8211; that means if you have a gaming console, you can watch Netflix from that device as well.</p>
<p>Gaming consoles work very much like a <a href="https://track.flexlinkspro.com/a.ashx?foid=1067927.2905429&amp;amp;foc=2&amp;amp;fot=9999&amp;amp;fos=1&amp;amp;fobs=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv&amp;amp;url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.roku.com%2Fproducts%2Fplayers">Roku</a> or similar streaming device; when you&#8217;re tired of games, you can log into Netflix via the on-screen app.</p>

<h2>Watch Netflix by &#8220;casting&#8221; it from your phone to a TV</h2>
<a href="https://static.businessinsider.com/image/572a532b910584cc008c0d20.jpg" rel="modal"><img src="https://static.businessinsider.com/image/572a532b910584cc008c0d20-1200.jpg" class="size_primary" alt=""></a><p class="image_source">Google</p>If you have a Chromecast device, you can broadcast Netflix from your mobile device or computer to your TV.<p>If you have Netflix on your smartphone, perhaps you&#8217;d like to get the video from the phone to your TV. Luckily, there are devices that make that easy.<a href="https://www.amazon.com/Google-H2G2-42-Chromecast-Ultra/dp/B0157OY5EA/?&amp;amp;_encoding=UTF8&amp;amp;tag=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv-20&amp;amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;amp;linkId=e1b704cbdff9d0194c33829dc7b7af62&amp;amp;camp=1789&amp;amp;creative=9325">Google Chromecast</a> is a simple media player that connects to your TV and lets you wirelessly send anything that&#8217;s on your phone to your television.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.businessinsider.com.au/what-is-google-chromecast-2019-4">Chromecast</a> calls this &#8220;casting,&#8221; and you can use this to watch Netflix at full resolution on your TV. In a similar way, if you have an Apple TV, you can wirelessly connect your phone using Bluetooth and display video like Netflix on your television.</p>

<h2>Watch Netflix by connecting a laptop or mobile device to your TV</h2>
<a href="https://static.businessinsider.com/image/5cf5389311e2050a16406ccf.jpg" rel="modal"><img src="https://static.businessinsider.com/image/5cf5389311e2050a16406ccf-1200.jpg" class="size_primary" alt=""></a><p class="image_source">Dave Johnson/Business Insider</p>Using a connection cable, you can display the Netflix from your phone, tablet, or laptop on your TV.<p>Finally, with the right cable, you can connect your computer or mobile device to a television to project video on the bigger screen. The trick, of course, is having the right cable. In most cases, you&#8217;ll want to be able to connect your device to the TV with an <a href="https://www.amazon.com/AmazonBasics-High-Speed-HDMI-Cable-1-Pack/dp/B014I8SSD0/ref=sr_1_3?keywords=HDMI+cable&amp;amp;qid=1559578714&amp;amp;s=electronics&amp;amp;sr=1-3&amp;amp;_encoding=UTF8&amp;amp;tag=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv-20&amp;amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;amp;linkId=710981557d3f6d2a99c898032f53b43e&amp;amp;camp=1789&amp;amp;creative=9325">HDMI cable</a>, so your laptop should have an HDMI output that you can connect to the HDMI input on your TV.</p>
<p>If you have an older laptop (and television), you may be able to use an old-fashioned <a href="https://www.amazon.com/AmazonBasics-DVI-Cable-Feet-Meters/dp/B00IHMFIBY/ref=sr_1_3?keywords=DVI+cable&amp;amp;qid=1559578795&amp;amp;s=electronics&amp;amp;sr=1-3&amp;amp;_encoding=UTF8&amp;amp;tag=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv-20&amp;amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;amp;linkId=0fd298c2c88d2500046c80c27695981d&amp;amp;camp=1789&amp;amp;creative=9325">DVI cable</a> instead. To connect your iPhone or iPad to the TV, you&#8217;ll need a <a href="http://apple.sjv.io/c/196318/435031/7613?subId1=bi_060319_get-netflix-on-tv&amp;amp;u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fshop%2Fproduct%2FMD826AM%2FA%2Flightning-digital-av-adapter%3Ffnode%3D91%26fs%3Df%253Dadapter-cable%2526fh%253D4588%25252B45b0%25252B45c4">Lightning Digital AV Adaptor</a>. For more info, check out our article, &#8220;<strong><a href="https://www.businessinsider.com.au/how-to-connect-iphone-to-tv-2019-4">How to connect your iPhone to a TV in two different ways</a></strong>.&#8221;</p>
