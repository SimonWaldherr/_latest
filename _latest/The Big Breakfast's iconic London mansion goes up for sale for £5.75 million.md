 <p>     </p> <p>Seven months after the show was axed, the house was damaged by a fire which destroyed a large part of the first floor and the roof.</p> <p>The house was saved and completely renovated and has been turned into a family home.</p> <p>Set by the side of a canal, a short distance from the Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park, the house boasts a huge swimming pool in the garden.</p>
