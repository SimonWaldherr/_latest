<div><p id="speakable-summary">Usually, databases about companies have to be painstakingly updated by humans. <a href="https://www.soleadify.com/">Soleadify</a> is a startup that uses machine learning to create profiles for businesses in any industry. The first of the company&#8217;s products is a business search engine that keeps over 40 million business profiles updated, currently used by hundreds of companies in the USA, Europe and Asia for sales and marketing activities.</p>
<p>It&#8217;s now secured $1.5M in seed round funding from European venture firms <a href="https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/gapminder-vc">GapMinder</a> Venture Partners and <a href="https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/day-one-capital">DayOne Capital</a>, as well as several prominent business angels, through <a href="https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/seedblink">Seedblink</a>, an equity crowdfunding platform based out of Bucharest Romania.</p>
<p>The company plans to use the funds to further improve their technology, build partnerships and expand their marketing capabilities.</p><p class="piano-inline-promo"></p>
<p>On top of Soleadify&#8217;s data, they build solutions for prospecting, market research, customer segmentation and industry monitoring.</p>
<p>The way it&#8217;s done is by frequently scanning billions of webpages, identifying and classifying relevant data points and creating connections between them. The result is a database of business data, that is normally only available through laborious, manual research.</p>
