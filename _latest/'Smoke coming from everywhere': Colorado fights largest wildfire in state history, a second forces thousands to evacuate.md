<div><p class="teal-video-wrap"></p><p class="gnt_em_vt"></p><p class="gnt_em_vb gnt_em_vb__te"></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Colorado crews scrambled Monday to gain ground against two blazes &#8211; including the largest in state history &#8211; as this year's historic wildfire season imparted&#160;more heartbreak and hardships on&#160;the West.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">The <a href="https://inciweb.nwcg.gov/incident/6964/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Cameron Peak Fire</a>, which has torched more than 203,000 acres&#160;through the Arapaho and Roosevelt National Forests near Cameron Pass and Chambers Lake in&#160; Larimer County, was 62% contained as of Monday morning. Gusty winds and dry conditions&#160;have fueled the growth of the fire, which ignited in August,&#160;according to the the U.S.&#160;Forest Service said.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Cloudy conditions and poor visibility kept fire aircraft grounded, but the weather also aided in making progress on a few hot spots, fire officials said.&#160;&#160;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">The Larimer County Sheriff's Office&#160;assembled 10&#160;assessment teams to tally the damage of the fire's&#160;most recent pushes, Sheriff Justin Smith&#160;wrote in <a href="https://www.facebook.com/SheriffJustinSmith/posts/3409932005761951" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">a Facebook post Monday morning</a>.</p><p id="~ad-slot-7103-usatoday-native-article_link-news-national-2" class="gnt_x_sl gnt_x_al"></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Another blaze, the <a href="https://inciweb.nwcg.gov/incident/7245/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Calwood Fire</a>&#160;raging in the north-central part of the state, forced nearly 3,000 people to flee &#8211; including residents of tiny&#160;Jamestown at the foothills of the Rockies&#160;&#8211; over the weekend. The fire, which ignited Saturday near the Cal-Wood Education Center&#160;about 17 miles from downtown Boulder, has singed&#160;more than 8,700 acres&#160;and is 15% contained.&#160;&#160;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">At least 26 homes&#160;have been lost to the blaze, which is being driven by ferocious winds, said&#160;Mike Wagner, division chief with the&#160;Boulder County Sheriff's Office.&#160;The National Center for Atmospheric Research&#8217;s Mesa lab recorded gusts of 59 mph on Saturday.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p"><strong class="gnt_ar_b_al">'It just exploded':&#160;</strong><a href="https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/nation/2020/10/18/colorado-wildfires-thousands-flee-cameron-peak-calwood-fires/3702819001/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Cameron Peak, CalWood fires drive thousands from their homes in Colorado</a></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Mary Ann&#160;Beard told the <a href="https://www.dailycamera.com/2020/10/18/im-just-sort-of-numb-cal-wood-fire-evacuees-brace-for-the-worst/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Daily Camera</a> she has been forced to evacuate her&#160;Mountain Ridge home five times. When she&#160;got a call&#160;Saturday, she assumed the order was for the Cameron Peak Fire, not realizing the Cal-Wood blaze was burning less than 15 miles away.&#160;</p><img class="gnt_em_img_i" alt="Flames from the Cameron Peak Fire, the largest wildfire in Colorado history, burn trees along a ridge outside Estes Park, Colo., on Oct. 16, 2020."><p class="gnt_em_img_ccw gnt_em_img_ccw__cap gnt_em_img_ccw__crd"></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">&#8220;I&#8217;ve learned to just have a duffle bag open in the closet to just throw clothes into it,&#8221; she told the newspaper. &#8220;And that&#8217;s what happened.&#8221;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Beard said 17&#160;homes dotted her subdivision, "and now there's only one standing.&#160;There&#8217;s just foundations and smoke coming from everywhere. I don&#8217;t think it&#8217;s all hit me yet.&#8221; &#160;&#160;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Courtney Walsh <a href="https://www.denverpost.com/2020/10/18/calwood-fire-update-sunday-evacuations/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">told the Denver Post</a> she&#160;had about a 30-minute warning to gather&#160;her children, animals and belongings before sheriff&#8217;s deputies urged her to leave her&#160;house near Boulder on Saturday afternoon.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">&#8220;We were just waiting and waiting, and you know there is Twitter and live feeds,&#8221; she said. &#8220;Then we saw a picture from the National Weather Service, and it focused on our house burning. So then we knew."</p><p id="~ad-slot-7103-usatoday-native-article_link-news-national-3" class="gnt_x_sl gnt_x_al"></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">The Colorado wildfires were among more than 60 major fires burning in 11 Western states, according to the National Interagency Fire Center.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p"><strong class="gnt_ar_b_al">What we know:</strong><a href="https://www.coloradoan.com/story/news/2020/10/19/cameron-peak-fire-live-coverage-monday-what-we-know-fire/3706158001/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">&#160;Cameron Peak Fire crews 'make good progress' during cloudy day Sunday</a></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">In hard-hit California, more than a dozen wildfires have raged in recent months.&#160;But there was some good news: The White House and the Federal Emergency Management Agency approved the state's request for&#160;disaster relief funds&#160;to clean up damage from six blazes.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">In Utah, two new fires were much smaller but also forced evacuations. The Range Fire had burned 3,000 acres and was not at all contained; the&#160;Fire Canyon Fire was estimated at 1,460 acres&#160;but only&#160;10% contained.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">And in Wyoming, the Mullen Fire had&#160;burned 176,386 acres&#160;in the&#160;Medicine Bow National Forest west of Laramie.&#160;The blaze, which sparked Sept. 17, was 55% contained.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Contributing: John Bacon, USA TODAY; Sarah Kyle, Fort Collins Coloradoan; The Associated Press&#160;</p><a class="gnt_em gnt_em__lp gnt_em_gl gnt_em__el" href="/picture-gallery/news/nation/2020/10/18/colorado-wildfires-force-evacuations/3705872001/"><img class="gnt_em_gl_i" alt="" width="660" height="372"><p class="gnt_em_gl_i_nt"></p><p class="gnt_em_gl_vg"></p><p class="gnt_em_t gnt_em_t__hb"></p></a></div>
