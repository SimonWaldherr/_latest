<p>The occasion was quarterback Joe Flacco getting sacked by host Miami in the <a href="https://nypost.com/2020/10/18/jets-look-completely-inept-in-shutout-loss-to-dolphins/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">24-0 defeat</a>, but there were plenty of other spirit-destroying moments that could have produced the same reaction.</p><p>The Jets (0-6) are now the only team without a victory and are a solid candidate to match the 0-16 ineptitude of the 2008 Detroit Lions and 2017 Cleveland Browns.</p><p>They are <a href="https://www.teamrankings.com/nfl/stat/points-per-game" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">last out of 32 teams in the NFL</a> in points per game (12.5) and 29th in <a href="https://www.teamrankings.com/nfl/stat/opponent-points-per-game" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">points allowed</a> (30.8).</p><p>&#8220;It sucks,&#8221; head coach Adam Gase told the New York Post after the Miami game. &#8220;Everybody is just trying to do everything they can to win a game and just haven&#8217;t been able to do it.&#8221;</p><p>On the plus side, the more the Jets lose, the better chance they have of drafting coveted Clemson quarterback Trevor Lawrence.</p><p>Whether <a href="https://www.si.com/nfl/2020/10/19/trevor-lawrence-new-york-jets-eli-manning" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">he would want to go there</a> is another story.</p><p>The Jets have not made the playoffs since 2010 and <a href="http://www.jt-sw.com/football/pro/teams.nsf/histories/jets" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">have won just 21 games</a> in nearly 4 1/2 seasons.</p><p>Check out some reactions to Douglas:</p><blockquote class="twitter-tweet" align="center"><p lang="en" dir="ltr">I think Jets GM Joe Douglas really only wants one finger up.  <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/FireGase?src=hash&amp;ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">#FireGase</a> <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/TakeFlight?src=hash&amp;ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">#TakeFlight</a> <a href="https://t.co/EVAc498PRC">pic.twitter.com/EVAc498PRC</a></p>&#8212;
