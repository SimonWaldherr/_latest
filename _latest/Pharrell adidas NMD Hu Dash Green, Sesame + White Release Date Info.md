<p>Expanding on their collaborations, <a href="https://www.sneakerfiles.com/sneakers/pharrell/">Pharrell</a> and adidas Originals will debut their new NMD Hu lineup in specific regions.</p>
<p>First, we have the &#8216;Dash Green&#8217; pair that will be limited to 7,777 pairs and releases globally. The &#8216;Sesame&#8217; is launching in North America while the &#8216;White&#8217; pair will be exclusive to the Asia-Pacific. </p>
<p>The trio of <a href="https://www.sneakerfiles.com/sneakers/adidas-nmd-human-race/">adidas NMD Hu</a> releases comes constructed with engineered mesh, the standard embroidered text on the mid-foot that translates to &#8216;Human Race.&#8217; Finally, we have the standard lace cage, Boost midsole, and Black rubber outsole.</p>
<h2>Pharrell adidas NMD Hu 2020 Release Details</h2>
<p>The three Pharrell x adidas NMD Hu colorways will release on October 25th. The &#8216;Dash Green&#8217; pair will debut on <a href="https://www.adidas.com/Pharrell" class="external" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">adidas.com/Pharrell</a> and at other select shops. The &#8216;Sesame&#8217; iteration will launch in North America on Confirmed while the &#8216;White&#8217; pair will release exclusively in the Asia-Pacific. The retail price is $220 each. See more images below that will give you a closer look.</p>
