<p>The 45-year-old rapper on Monday urged his social media followers to &#8220;VOTE For TRUMP&#8221; after seeing figures related to Joe Biden&#8217;s tax plan.</p>
<p>The Queens-bred artist bashed the Democratic presidential nominee&#8217;s plan, which would raise tax rates for corporations and people making $400,000 or more per year.</p>
<p>He posted a photo from what looked like a CNBC broadcast, <a href="https://www.cnbc.com/2020/10/19/tax-rates-could-top-60percent-und.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">citing studies</a> stating that under the plan, high earners in New York City could face combined federal and state tax rates of 62 percent.</p>
<p>&#8220;WHAT THE F&#8211;K!&#8221; he wrote. &#8220;(VOTE ForTRUMP) IM OUT, F&#8211;K NEW YORK.&#8221;</p>
<p>He added: &#8220;The KNICKS never win anyway.&#8221;</p>
<p>50 Cent then wrote: &#8220;I don&#8217;t care Trump doesn&#8217;t like black people 62% are you out of ya f&#8211;ing mind.&#8221;</p>
<p>The endorsement came after the rapper in 2016 posted that he hoped Trump wouldn&#8217;t win the presidential election.</p>
