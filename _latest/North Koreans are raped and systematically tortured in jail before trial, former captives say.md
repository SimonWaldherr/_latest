  North Koreans are raped and systematically tortured in jail before trial, former captives say 
description: > 
  Mistreatment of detainees - beating with a stick or kicking - was 'especially harsh' in the early stages of pretrial detention, interviewees said.A new report by US-based group Human Rights Watch details the gruesome conditions in North Korea's pretrial detention centres since 2011, when Kim Jong Un came to power.Former detainees recounted being kept in a constant state of fear through beatings or being able to hear others being tortured in North Korea's pretrial detention system.Adams added that pretrial detention and investigation system was 'arbitrary, violent, cruel and degrading'.Pyongyang maintains that it protects and promotes 'genuine human rights', and says there is no justification for the West to try to set human rights standards for the rest of the world.
