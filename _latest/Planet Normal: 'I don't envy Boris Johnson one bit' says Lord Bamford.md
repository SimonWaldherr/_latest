<div><p>Chairman of JCB, Anthony Bamford, has said that he believes the Prime Minister has dealt well with the coronavirus pandemic&#160;and that he doesn't envy him "one bit" for having to deal with&#160;an unprecedented situation.&#160;</p>
<p>The long-standing Conservative donor said "don't forget there's no living Prime Minister, or President for that matter, that's gone through this".&#160;</p>
<p>Lord Bamford also told Liam Halligan on the Telegraph's Planet Normal podcast, which you can listen to easily on the audio player above, that "of course the government will have made mistakes as time has gone along, and they could still be making some mistakes but that in the main, I think he's done a very good job" and that Boris Johnson is "a great Prime Minister".</p>
