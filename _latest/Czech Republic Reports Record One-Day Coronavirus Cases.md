Apprimaxtemly 4,000 people are currently hospitalized. </p><p id="ac-lre-player"></p><p>As cases surge in the country, authorities are reinstating a strict mask mandate that was in place in spring and that was lifted over the summer. </p><p>Masks will now be mandatory anywhere in urban areas and cars, as well as indoors and on public transportation and at outdoor stations. </p><p>According to the European Centre for Disease Control, the Czech Republic has one of the worst infection rates in Europe, with more than 900 infections per 100,000 people, in the past 14 days.</p></div>
