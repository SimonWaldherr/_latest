UNCASVILLE, Conn. – Bellator returns to the States this week with the women’s featherweight title on the line. Bellator 249 takes place Thursday at Mohegan Sun Arena in Uncasville, Conn. The main card airs on CBS Sports Network following prelims on MMA Junkie.

Following Wednesday’s official Bellator 249 weigh-ins, which saw two fighters miss their marks on the scale, the athletes came face-to-face for some traditional pre-fight staredowns.

Check out the main card faceoffs below, including headliners Cris Cyborg and Arlene Blencowe, who fight for Cyborg’s title in the main event.
