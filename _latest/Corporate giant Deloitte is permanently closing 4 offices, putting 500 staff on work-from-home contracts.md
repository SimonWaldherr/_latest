<p>Global accounting and consulting firm Deloitte will close four of its 50 British offices as it reviews its real estate portfolio in the coronavirus pandemic, but will retain the staff on work-from-home contracts, it said on Saturday.</p><p>Deloitte&#160;said it would shut its offices in Gatwick, Liverpool, Nottingham and Southampton, where about 500 people work.</p><p>COVID-19 has changed working life for millions of people around the world, many of whom have switched from offices to working from home &#8212; reducing demand for office space and prompting companies to opt out of renewing leases.</p><p>"COVID-19 has fast-tracked our future of work programme, leading us to review our real estate portfolio," Stephen Griggs,&#160;Deloitte's UK managing partner, said in an emailed statement.</p>
