            </p><p>Doug Ford is the leader of the Progressive Conservative Party of Ontario and the 26th Premier of Ontario. He was first elected to Toronto city council to represent Ward 2 Etobicoke North in 2010, when his brother Rob Ford was elected Mayor of Toronto. Doug Ford ran for Mayor of Toronto in 2014, but lost to John Tory. Ford won the leadership of the Progressive Conservative Party of Ontario in March 2018.</p>
