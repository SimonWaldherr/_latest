<p>A US investigation found current and former Aeroflot Airlines employees helped to smuggle $50 million in stolen electronic devices to Russia.</p><p>The investigation, conducted by the FBI, Customs and Border Protection, the New York City Police Department, and other agencies, alleges 10 defendants of smuggling $50 million worth of electronic devices using current and former Aeroflot Airlines workers. The devices included iPhones, iPads, and Apple Watches.&#160;</p><p>An inspection of crew members' luggage found millions of dollars of electronic devices, "many of which" had been stolen, per a <a href="https://www.justice.gov/usao-edny/pr/ten-individuals-charged-50-million-russian-smuggling-scheme">release</a>. Federal officers arrested eight leaders of the scheme Monday afternoon, while two others are fugitives.</p><p>The Department of State has revoked 113 visas of Aeroflot employees in connection with the scheme.&#160;</p>

<p>"As alleged, the defendants were members of an international smuggling ring that used a network of operators here and in Russia to circumvent US export laws and regulations," United States Attorney DuCharme stated in a <a href="https://www.justice.gov/usao-edny/pr/ten-individuals-charged-50-million-russian-smuggling-scheme">release</a>.</p><p>The airline industry in particular has suffered from lost revenue and service this year as the COVID-19 pandemic stymied travel, per the <a href="https://www.iata.org/en/pressroom/pr/2020-03-05-01/">International Air Transport Association</a>. Aeroflot reported a loss of $485 million in <a href="https://www.reuters.com/article/us-russia-aeroflot-results/russias-aeroflot-falls-to-second-quarter-loss-as-air-traffic-tumbles-idUSKBN25R1B0">August</a>.</p><p>Police in the UK had arrested another former flight attendant for <a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/flight-attendant-deal-drugs-uk-pandemic-coronavirus-2020-9">dealing drugs after getting laid off</a>.</p><p>Aeroflot Airlines, one of the world's <a href="https://www.aeroflot.ru/us-en/about/aeroflot_today/aeroflot_history/history">oldest carriers</a>, is currently valued at $12.5 million.</p>
