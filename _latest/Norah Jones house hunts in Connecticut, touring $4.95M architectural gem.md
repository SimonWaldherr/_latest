<div><p>Singer Norah Jones is on the prowl for a country house. She recently checked out a $4.95 million property in Ridgefield, Conn.</p>
<p>The Arts and Crafts-style, 6,000-square-foot home at 323 Florida Hill Road sits on 29 acres. It&#8217;s owned by fashion designer Alexander Julian and his wife Meagan, who&#8217;ve entertained famous friends at the house, including Susan Sarandon, Tim Robbins and Michelle Williams.</p>
<p>&#8220;I&#8217;ll never forget Paul Newman standing at the front door with a birthday gift of a case of his favorite beer over his shoulder,&#8221; Julian tells Gimme.</p>
<p>He bought the property in 1988 for $1.6 million from the widow of Charlie Bluhdorn, who headed Gulf and Western. The house, by architect John Marsh Davis, features glass walls, a chef&#8217;s kitchen, vaulted ceilings, three fireplaces and 56 custom paint colors from artist Donald Kaufman. Outside, Jones might find inspiration in its pool, tennis courts, summer cottages, ponds and lush gardens.</p>
<p>The listing brokers are <a href="https://www.williampitt.com/our-agents/list/davideverson/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">David Everson and William Ayles of Sotheby&#8217;s International Realty</a>.</p>
