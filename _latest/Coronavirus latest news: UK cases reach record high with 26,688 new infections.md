<p>The UK's official coronavirus case tally&#160;has reached a new record high with 26,688 new cases confirmed today by the Department of Health.</p>
<p>The overall caseload now stands at 789,229, although it is believed that only a fraction of infections in the first wave of coronavirus were recorded.</p>
<p>A further 996 patients have been admitted to hospital, with 191 new deaths linked with coronavirus recorded in the last 24 hours, 94 of which were in English hospitals.</p>
<p>Talks about a move to Tier 3 lockdown restrictions in Tyneside and Tees Valley have been paused amid signs that progress has been made in controlling the spread of coronavirus.</p>
<p>Tier 3 discussions have also taken place in West Yorkshire and Nottinghamshire, and South Yorkshire today became the latest area to agree to the strict set of curbs.</p>
<p>It comes as the Government pledged to carry out an &#8220;urgent&#8221; review of the Covid-19 powers which allow its officials to use &#8220;reasonable force&#8221; in order to make people self-isolate.</p>
