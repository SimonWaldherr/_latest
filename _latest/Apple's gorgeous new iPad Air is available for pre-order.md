ICYMI: Apple announced the new iPhone 12 on Oct. 13 — but if you were more excited about the new iPad lineup Apple announced last month, the iPad Air is now available for pre-order.

The tablet comes in some fun new colors, including green and sky blue. Aside from a new look, this iPad runs on Apple's most powerful chip yet, the A14 Bionic, making it faster than ever. It boasts a 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display and it supports the Apple Pen and Magic Keyboard.

The iPad Air is available in five colors. Image: apple

This handheld machine can do more than some laptops are capable of. It's fast, has enhanced graphics, and shoots video in 4K. It's especially great for photo editing, audio editing, and more creative outlets.

The new iPad Air comes out on Oct. 23, but it's available for pre-order now from Apple's site as well as other retailers like Amazon and Best Buy. The Apple iPad Air starts at $599.
