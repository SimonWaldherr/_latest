<div><p>Thousands of American-flag-waving Trump supporters rallied in Newport Beach on Sunday as the president arrived <a class="link" href="https://www.latimes.com/politics/story/2020-10-18/trump-fundraiser-california">for a fundraiser</a> to bolster his reelection bid on Nov. 3.</p><p>Red MAGA hats were ubiquitous in the crowd, and masks were scant. The sound of country music mingled with shouts of, &#8220;Four more years!&#8221;</p>

They demanded that Trump take action against Turkey, which has expressed support for Azerbaijan.</p><p>&#8220;Armenia needs Trump!&#8221; a protester, his shoulders draped in the orange, red and blue Armenian flag, yelled into a megaphone. &#8220;Save Christian Armenia!&#8221; Many of the Armenian American demonstrators were also Trump supporters.</p><p>Melimeh Soukiasian, 36, of Los Angeles said she wasn&#8217;t sure yet who she would cast a vote for in November but said she would support Trump if he spoke out against Turkey&#8217;s actions.</p><p class="google-dfp-ad-caption">Advertisement</p>
