<div><p>Click to email this to a friend (Opens in new window)</p><p>Click to share on Twitter (Opens in new window)</p><p>Click to share on Facebook (Opens in new window)</p><p>Braunwyn Windham-Burke had experience with alcoholism long before she sought treatment for her own addiction struggles.</p>
<p>On Wednesday&#8217;s episode of &#8220;The Real Housewives of Orange County,&#8221; the 42-year-old candidly said that her father died from drinking, making the revelation during a conversation with new &#8220;RHOC&#8221; cast member Elizabeth Lyn Vargas, who first revealed that her dad was an alcoholic.</p>
<p>&#8220;My dad died from drinking,&#8221; Windham-Burke disclosed.</p>
<p>In a confessional she elaborated: &#8220;I feel like I&#8217;m being punched in the gut. My dad had a heart attack because he drank. Every day.&#8221;</p>
<p>Windham-Burke said she was two weeks sober at the time of their conversation.</p>
<p>During the on-screen chat, Vargas also said her mom was her &#8220;rock&#8221; and had to deal with a lot in her life.</p>
<p>Windham-Burke revealed in the Season 15 premiere that <a href="https://pagesix.com/2020/10/15/rhoc-star-braunwyn-windham-burke-says-shes-an-alcoholic/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">she is an alcoholic</a>, saying: &#8220;I don&#8217;t know if it&#8217;s divine intervention. I don&#8217;t know. But for the first time in my life, I can say, &#8216;My name is Braunwyn and I&#8217;m an alcoholic.&#8217;&#8221;</p>
<p>Windham-Burke opened up about her sobriety afterwards, <a href="https://pagesix.com/2020/10/15/lala-kent-braunwyn-windham-burke-and-leah-mcsweeney-open-up-about-sobriety/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">telling Glamour that she quit drinking</a> after celebrating her co-star Kelly Dodd&#8217;s now-husband Rick Leventhal&#8217;s birthday in Miami in January.</p>
<p>Windham-Burke has not discussed much of her relationship with her dad on the show; however, her mom, Dr. Deb, has made appearances.</p>
