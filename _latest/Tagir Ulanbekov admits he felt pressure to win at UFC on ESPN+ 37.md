ABU DHABI – Tagir Ulanbekov beat Bruno by unanimous decision Saturday to open up the preliminary card at UFC on ESPN+ 37 from Flash Forum on Yas Island.

Take a look inside the fight with Ulanbekov, a teammate of UFC lightweight champion Khabib Nurmagomedov, who pushed his overall winning streak to four.

Result: Tagir Ulanbekov def. Bruno Silva via unanimous decision (30-27, 29-28, 29-28)

Updated records: Tagir Ulanbekov (11-1 MMA, 1-0 UFC), Bruno Silva (10-6-2 MMA, 0-2 UFC)
