<div><p>Danish submarine killer Peter Madsen was on the loose in the western suburbs of Copenhagen on Tuesday morning, after mounting an audacious jail-break.</p>
<p>Madsen, who is serving a life sentence for the brutal murder of the Swedish journalist Kim Wall, reportedly threatened police that he was carrying a bomb and also may have wielded a pistol-like objects.</p>
<p>The Ekstra Bladet newspaper photographed the 49-year-old murderer sitting on the ground about 500 metres from Herstedvester Prison, surrounded by police.</p>
<p>In a statement, Copenhagen police said that a man had been arrested after a prison break but declined to identify the suspect.</p>
