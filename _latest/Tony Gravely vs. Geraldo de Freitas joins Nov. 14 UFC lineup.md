<div><p>A bantamweight matchup is the latest addition to the UFC&#8217;s Nov. 14 lineup.</p>
<p><a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/tony-gravely">Tony Gravely</a> (19-6 MMA, 0-1 UFC) will face <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/geraldo-de-freitas">Geraldo de Freitas</a> (12-5 MMA, 1-1 UFC) in the event that takes place at the UFC Apex in Las Vegas.</p>
<p>Two people with knowledge of the situation informed MMA Junkie of the booking Wednesday, but they anonymity since the promotion has yet to make an official announcement.</p>
<p>After earning a contract on &#8220;Dana White&#8217;s Contender Series&#8221; in August 2019, Gravely dropped his UFC debut when he was submitted by Brett Johns at UFC on ESPN+ 24 in January.</p>
<p>De Freitas has split his first two UFC appearances, picking up a win over Felipe Colares in February 2019, then getting edged out by Chris Gutierrez at UFC on ESPN+ 14 last August. He was scheduled to face Jack Shore at UFC on ESPN+ 29 in March, but the event was canceled due to the coronavirus outbreak.</p>
<p>With the addition, the&#160;UFC on ESPN+ 41&#160;lineup includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Louis Smolka vs. Jose Quinonez</li>
<li>Kanako Murata&#160;vs.&#160;Livinha Souza</li>
<li>Kay Hansen&#160;vs.&#160;Cory McKenna</li>
<li>Julian Marquez&#160;vs. Saparbek Safarov</li>
<li>Miranda Granger&#160;vs.&#160;Ashley Yoder</li>
<li>Gabriel Benitez vs. Justin Jaynes</li>
<li>Antonio Arroyo vs. Andreas Michailidis</li>
<li>Zarrukh Adashev vs. Jeff Molina</li>
<li>Don&#8217;Tale Mayes vs. Roque Martinez</li>
<li>Rhys McKee vs. Alex Morono</li>
<li>Tony Gravely vs. Geraldo de Freitas</li>
</ul>
