<div><p class="mol-para-with-font">Pope Francis has endorsed same-sex civil unions for the first time since taking the papal role.&#160;</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">The approval came&#160;midway through a feature-length documentary, titled Francesco, which had its premiere at the Rome Film Festival earlier&#160;today.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">The film delves into issues Francis cares about most, including the environment, poverty, migration, racial and income inequality, and the people most affected by discrimination.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">'Homosexual people have the right to be in a family. They are children of God. Nobody should be thrown out or be made miserable over it,' Francis said in one of his sit-down interviews for the film.&#160;</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">'What we have to have is a civil union law; that way they are legally covered.'</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">The Pope's remarks will come as a shock to millions of Roman Catholics who have long followed the doctrine that gay relationships are sinful and accepted the Church's stand against the worldwide advance of gay rights.&#160;</p><img id="i-21fbefc140afe8ce" <p class="mol-para-with-font">The Catholic Church teaches that homosexual tendencies are not sinful but that homosexual acts are.&#160;</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">It also instructs that&#160;homosexuals should be treated with dignity.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">Francis's predecessors, including Benedict XVI and&#160;John Paul II, condemned&#160;same-sex marriage during their papal tenure.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">Francis himself had opposed legislation to approve same-sex marriages&#160;in Argentina when he was Archbishop of Buenos Aires&#160;a decade ago - but had&#160;supported some kind of legal protection for the rights of gay couples at the time.&#160;</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">However, shortly after becoming Pope, he said of gay people that 'we must be brothers'.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">He added: 'If a person is gay and seeks God and has goodwill, who am I to judge him?'&#160;</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">Papal biographer to cause any trauma for the children.&#160;</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">It was not clear in which country Rubera lives.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">Rubera said the pope telephoned him
