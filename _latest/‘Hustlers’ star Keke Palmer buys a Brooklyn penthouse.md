<div><p>Actress, <a href="https://nypost.com/2019/09/07/keke-palmer-dishes-on-strippers-r-kelly-and-co-hosting-gma/">TV personality and singer Keke Palmer</a>, who <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/B_jJtXNjM0_/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">dyed her hair orange</a> during the COVID-19 lockdown, just scored some roomy new Williamsburg digs.</p>
<p>Palmer &#8212; who got her start playing Queen Latifah&#8217;s niece in &#8220;Barbershop 2: Back in Business&#8221; and co-starred with Jennifer Lopez last year in &#8220;Hustlers&#8221; &#8212; just <a href="https://streeteasy.com/sale/1379158" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">bought a 1,482-square-foot penthouse</a> on North 7th Street for $2.35 million (repped by <a href="https://www.elliman.com/newyorkcity/associate/527-a-df19030719265265429/mmonica-novo" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Monica Novo of Douglas Elliman</a>).</p>
<p>The three-bedroom, two-and-a-half bathroom abode &#8212; which boasts high ceilings, wide-plank oak floors, an open chef&#8217;s kitchen and two outdoor terraces &#8212; was asking $2.49 million with Douglas Elliman listing broker Tamir Shemesh. Palmer&#8217;s luxurious new home is in a nine-story building by design darling Paris Forino; amenities include a landscaped garden, a common roof area, a penthouse lounge and private cabanas.</p>
