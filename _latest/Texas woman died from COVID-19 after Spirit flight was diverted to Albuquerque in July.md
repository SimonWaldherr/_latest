He also noted that she did have underlying health conditions.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">He said the woman, who lived in Garland, Texas,&#160;had difficulty breathing and was given oxygen but died on the jetway in Albuquerque, NBC reported.&#160;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p"><strong>CATCH UP:</strong> <a class="gnt_ar_b_a">Arizona reports more than 1,000 new COVID-19 cases and 7 new&#160;deaths</a></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">On Tuesday, Lauren Trimble, Jenkins' chief of staff, told The Arizona ground, using onboard medical kits and personal protective equipment, and receiving assistance from credentialed medical personnel traveling on the flight."</p><p
