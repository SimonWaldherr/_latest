<div><p>What the cluck!</p>
<p>Forget the $10,000 Birkin bag, this season&#8217;s hottest handbag is a rubber chicken &#8220;henbag&#8221; that sells for just $32.95.</p>
<p>Sold on Amazon as the &#8220;Rubber Chicken Purse,&#8221; the accessory has received over 1,062 recommendations &#8212; mostly five stars &#8212; and while it&#8217;s not new, is making a comeback in fashion circles. The product description notes &#8220;Heads will turn as you carry this Henbag. It is sure to bring a smile to yourself and others each time you carry it. Fun gift!&#8221;</p>
<p>The purse is so beloved, an owner created an Instagram for the bag called <a href="https://www.instagram.com/afowlpurse/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">A Fowl Purse</a> which describes itself as documenting &#8220;the many adventures of Chicken, a rubber chicken purse living the dream in Northern California.&#8221;</p>
<p><a name="4b7f27d9d440bbbbd578f887a5604053"></a>	
	
	 
	</p>
<p>Meanwhile, the hashtag <a href="https://www.instagram.com/explore/tags/chickenpurse/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">#chickenpurse</a> has racked up over 3,000 posts on the social media site, with one user, <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/CGLETIuBx-x/?utm_source=ig_web_copy_link" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">@hello_cathyduarte</a>, noting: &#8220;Years ago I was cutting this lady&#8217;s hair and she had this rubber chicken purse l fell in love with it and finally got one it in May so now I&#8217;m switching out my bags and using this one for National handbag day&#8221;.</p>
<p>Another fan is so obsessed they got a <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/B4tQfbvA3Zy/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">tattoo</a> of the chicken purse.</p>
