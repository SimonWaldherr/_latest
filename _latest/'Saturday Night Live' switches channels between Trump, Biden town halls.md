<div><p class="endmarkEnabled">Jim Carrey returned to "Saturday Night Live" as Joe Biden in a satiric take on this week's town halls with President Donald Trump and challenger Joe Biden on different networks by switching channels between the two.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">"Flipping back and forth trying to decide between a Hallmark movie and an alien autopsy." the introduction said.</p><p></p><p id="taboolaReadMoreBelow"></p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Biden introduced himself by asking a small audience, likened to a poorly attended college lecture, "Who's ready to have some fun with facts and figures?"</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Asked about how he would handle the Covid-19 pandemic, Biden said, "Here&#8217;s the deal. Unlike the president I actually have a plan."</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">"What is it?" a member of the audience asked.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">"It&#8217;s a detailed proposal for doing or achieving something," Biden said.</p><p></p><p class="endmarkEnabled">At the Trump event, moderator Savannah Guthrie, played by Kate McKinnon, introduced herself as, "<a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/opinions/2020/10/16/trump-needed-factual-dressing-down-savannah-guthrie-obliged/" target="_blank">Surprise badass</a> Savannah Guthrie."</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">She suggested that, because he's president, Trump, played by Alec Baldwin, shouldn't be retweeting wild conspiracy theories.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">"You&#8217;re not just someone&#8217;s crazy uncle," Guthrie said.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">"Really," Baldwin's Trump said, "because this conversation we&#8217;re having right now is a preview of Thanksgiving dinner."</p><p></p><p class="endmarkEnabled">A quick switch back to Biden's channel had the former vice president in mid-sentence. "And that brings us back to 1939, a year I went to the world&#8217;s fair," he said.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Back at the Trump town hall, Guthrie had one last question for the president: "Why won&#8217;t you release <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/donald-trump/trump-lashes-out-new-york-times-report-alleging-years-tax-n1241220" target="_blank">your taxes</a>?"</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">"That&#8217;s simple," Trump said. "Because I don&#8217;t want to go to jail."</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Biden's parting words included this vow: "If elected I promise, I promise I won&#8217;t tweet once, because I don&#8217;t know how."</p></div>
