<div><p class="teal-video-wrap"></p><p class="gnt_em_vt"></p><p class="gnt_em_vb gnt_em_vb__te"></p><h2 class="gnt_ar_b_h2">The claim: Kamala Harris failed the bar exam on her first attempt, while Amy Coney Barrett graduated at the top of her law school class</h2><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Some of the most-watched moments from Judge Amy Coney Barrett's testimony before the Senate Judiciary Committee were her&#160;exchanges with Sen. Kamala Harris.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">One user took to Facebook to compare the qualifications of the conservative Supreme Court&#160;nominee and the Democratic vice presidential nominee.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">"Kamala Harris failed the bar 1st time. Amy Coney Barrett 1st in her class," the <a href="https://www.facebook.com/sherri.damico.5/posts/10223042854841485" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">post</a> read atop a background of laughter&#160;emojis. "I rest."</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">The user behind the post did not respond to a request from USA TODAY for comment.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p"><strong class="gnt_ar_b_al">Fact check:</strong><a href="https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/factcheck/2020/10/15/fact-check-photo-maskless-democratic-senators-2018-hearing/3657973001/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Photo of maskless Democratic senators is from 2018 Kavanaugh confirmation hearing</a></p><img class="gnt_em_img_i" alt="Sen. Kamala Harris highlighted the impact Barrett could have on a woman's right to an abortion on the second day of confirmation hearings."><p class="gnt_em_img_ccw gnt_em_img_ccw__cap gnt_em_img_ccw__crd"></p><h2 class="gnt_ar_b_h2">Both facts are true</h2><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">It's true that Harris failed the bar exam&#160;on her first attempt, per The<a href="https://www.nytimes.com/2016/05/29/magazine/kamala-harris-a-top-cop-in-the-era-of-black-lives-matter.html?_r=0" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a"> New York Times</a>. She later passed.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">The bar exam is the qualifying examination for lawyers to receive a license to practice law&#160;in a given state.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">California has a notoriously difficult bar exam. In 1985, the <a href="https://www.latimes.com/archives/la-xpm-1985-01-20-mn-10438-story.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Los Angeles Times</a> wrote that its pass rate "has generally hovered around 50%."</p><p id="~ad-slot-7103-usatoday-native-article_link-news-2" class="gnt_x_sl gnt_x_al"></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Harris graduated from the University of California, Hastings College of the Law&#160;in 1989.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Even now, the pass rate on the California Bar Examination averages out to&#160;approximately&#160;50%.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">In February,&#160;just 40% of "first-timers" passed,&#160;per&#160;a <a href="http://www.calbar.ca.gov/Portals/0/documents/FEB2020-CBX-Statistics.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">report</a> by&#160;the State Bar of California. In July 2019, around 60% of "first-timers" passed, per <a href="http://www.calbar.ca.gov/Portals/0/documents/July2019-CBX-Statistics.pdf?" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">another&#160;report</a>.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Harris once told&#160;a&#160;recent law school grad who didn't pass the test, "It&#8217;s not a measure of your capacity,&#8221; The New York Times also reported in 2016.</p><p id="~ad-slot-7103-usatoday-native-article_link-news-3" class="gnt_x_sl gnt_x_al"></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">It's also true that Barrett graduated at the top of her class at Notre Dame Law School, per <a href="https://www.politico.com/news/2020/09/20/amy-coney-barrett-supreme-court-419219" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Politico</a>.&#160;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p"><strong class="gnt_ar_b_al">Fact check:</strong><a href="https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/factcheck/2020/10/15/fact-check-trump-tweet-supreme-court-deciding-elections-fake/3573638001/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Fake Trump tweet says Supreme Court should decide every election</a></p><h2 class="gnt_ar_b_h2">Our rating: True</h2><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Based on our research, the claims about both&#160;Harris and Barrett are&#160;TRUE. Kamala Harris failed the bar exam on her first try, and Amy Coney Barrett graduated law school&#160;at the top of her class.</p><h2 class="gnt_ar_b_h2">Our fact-check sources:</h2><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Thank you for supporting our journalism.
