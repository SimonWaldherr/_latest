<p class="mol-para-with-font">Doctors are still baffled by 'long Covid'. Most people who contract the virus will recover within a fortnight after suffering a fever, cough and losing their sense of smell or taste for several days.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">But class="mol-para-with-font">They found that weight played a significant role in people's risk of having lasting symptoms, with sufferers having a slightly higher BMI than average.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">One in seven people from all age groups experienced symptoms of 'long Covid' for at least four weeks after they had beaten off the virus.</p><p
