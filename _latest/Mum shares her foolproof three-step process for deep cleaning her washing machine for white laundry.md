  Mum shares her foolproof three-step process for deep cleaning her washing machine for white laundry 
description: > 
  A Melbourne mum has shared her foolproof three-step process for deep cleaning her washing machine.You can do this in the sink or just in the washing machine with a wet cloth covered in dishwashing liquid.Spray the inside of the washing machine and wipe the seals with a homemade solution of white vinegar and five drops of tea tree oil.Then, add a cup of white vinegar into the dispenser and run your machine on empty at the hottest setting.Add the towels to the washing machine, along with liquid detergent and half a cup of plain, white vinegar.
