  WH considers axing federal funds to 'anarchist cities' of New York, D.C, Portland and Seattle 
description: > 
  Donald Trump is considering cutting federal funding for coronavirus relief and other aid programs for the poor and sick in cities he has deemed 'anarchist', including New York City; Portland, Oregon; Washington, D.C.; and Seattle, Washington.The cities could lose millions in funding for HIV treatment, screenings for newborns, programs related to the COVID-19 pandemic, along with nearly 200 other health programs, according to details in documents obtained and revealed by Politico Wednesday.'This is nothing more than political retribution,' a spokesperson for New York City Mayor Bill de Blasio, Laura Feyer, told Politico.The review of documents shows HHS sent its list of more than 1,500 funding awards for the targeted cities to the office last Friday.The short list of programs related to law enforcement under HHS jurisdiction include some public health initiatives, child support enforcement and hospital emergency preparedness.
