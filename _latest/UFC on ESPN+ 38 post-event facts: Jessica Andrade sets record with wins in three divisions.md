<div><p>The penultimate event of the UFC&#8217;s current &#8220;Fight Island&#8221; stretch took place Saturday with UFC on ESPN+ 38 at Flash Forum at Yas Island in Abu Dhabi. The entire lineup streamed on ESPN+.</p>
<p>The main event saw <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/brian-ortega">Brian Ortega</a> (15-1 MMA, 7-1 UFC) re-emerge as a featherweight title contender when he shut down <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/chan-sung-jung">Chan Sung Jung</a> (16-6 MMA, 6-3 UFC) over five rounds to <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/2020/10/ufc-espn-plus-38-results-brian-ortega-dominant-decision-long-layoff-chan-sung-jung-korean-zombie">win a unanimous decision</a> and get his hand raised for the first time in more than two years.</p>
<p>Ortega wasn&#8217;t the only one to make an impression on the card, though. For more on the numbers, check below for 35 post-event facts to come out of UFC on ESPN+ 38.</p>

<p>The UFC Promotional Guidelines Compliance payout for the event totaled $127,500.</p>
<p>Debuting fighters went 2-1 at the event.</p>
<p><a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/jimmy-crute">Jimmy Crute</a>, <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/jessica-andrade">Jessica Andrade</a>, <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/guram-kutateladze">Guram Kutateladze</a> and <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/mateusz-gamrot">Mateusz Gamrot</a> earned $50,000 UFC on ESPN+ 38 <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/lists/ufc-espn-plus-38-bonuses-jimmy-crute-knockout-jessica-andrade-fight-island">fight-night bonuses</a>.</p>
<p>UFC on ESPN+ 38 drew an announced attendance of zero for a live gate of $0.</p>
<p>Betting favorites went 6-5 on the card.</p>
<p>Betting favorites fell to 21-11 in UFC headliners this year.</p>
<p>Total fight time for the 11-bout card was 2:12:45.</p>

<p>Andrade became the 12th fighter in UFC history to earn victories in three weight classes. She&#8217;s the first female to accomplish the feat.</p>
<p><a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/katlyn-chookagian">Katlyn Chookagian</a> (14-4 MMA, 7-4 UFC) fell to 5-3 since she dropped to the UFC flyweight division in January 2018.</p>
<p>Chookagian has suffered both of her career stoppage losses by knockout.</p>

<p>Silva has suffered both of his career losses by decision.</p>
