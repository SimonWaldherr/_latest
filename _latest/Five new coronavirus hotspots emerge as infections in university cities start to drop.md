 <p>     </p> <p>Knowsley has the second highest rate, but this is down from 729.1 to 673.5, with 1,016 new cases.</p> <p>Liverpool is in third place, where the rate has dropped from 684.7 to 606.6, with 3,021 new cases.</p> <p>Case rates have also fallen in the cities of Newcastle, Exeter, Sheffield, Manchester and Leeds.</p> <p>The figures, for the seven days to October 15, are based on tests carried out in laboratories and in the wider community.</p> <p>The rate is expressed as the number of new cases per 100,000 people.</p>
