<div><p>A 29-year-old man was fatally stabbed in Brooklyn on Monday afternoon, police said.</p>
<p>The victim was found with several wounds lying on the sidewalk off East 18th Street near Church Avenue in Flatbush at about 2:40 p.m., authorities said.</p>
<p>The man was taken to a nearby hospital, but could not be saved, according to police.</p>
<p>No arrests were made in the fatal stabbing as of early Tuesday morning, cops said.</p>
