<div><p>Click to email this to a friend (Opens in new window)</p><p>Kim Kardashian has secretly settled a $6.1 million lawsuit with the security company who &#8220;failed to protect&#8221; her during her devastating robbery in Paris, <a href="https://www.thesun.co.uk/tvandshowbiz/12970596/kim-kardashian-settles-6-million-lawsuit-bodyguard-paris-robbery/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">according to the Sun. </a></p>
<p>In October 2016, Kardashian was <a href="https://pagesix.com/tag/kim-kardashian-robbed-in-paris/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">tied up and put in the bathtub of her hotel room</a> by five armed robbers who had posed as cops to gain entry. They left with $10 million worth of jewelry, including a $5 million diamond ring from her husband, Kanye West.</p>
<p>Her bodyguard, Pascal Duvier, had left her alone in the room while he escorted her sisters, Kourtney Kardashian and Kendall Jenner, to a nightclub. According to the British paper, Kim fired Duvier and then sued him and his company for negligence.</p>
<p>Reps for the reality star didn&#8217;t respond to our request for comment.</p>
