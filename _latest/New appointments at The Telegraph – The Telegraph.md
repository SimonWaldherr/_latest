<div><p dir="ltr">Kath Brown has been appointed Head of Weekend Lifestyle at The Telegraph.</p>
<p dir="ltr">In her new role, Kath will be responsible for editing and overseeing the Saturday and Sunday print lifestyle sections, as well as overseeing digital content for key lifestyle genres.&#160; Reporting in to Jane Bruton, Kath will take over the role from Paul Davies, who is appointed to the new role of Head of Family at The Telegraph.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Kath has extensive experience as a journalist and editor in women magazines and national newspapers.&#160; She started her career on teenage titles before joining Elle, as Deputy Editor. &#160; She was the launch editor of Red magazine and also worked as Associate Editor of Marie Claire and Editor of Woman &amp; Home.&#160; Kath will be joining The Telegraph in the new year.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>Jane Bruton, Deputy Editor &amp; Director of Lifestyle, The Telegraph</strong> said: &#8216;This is a pivotal role in the lifestyle team overseeing the content that sits at the heart of our reader&#8217;s weekend.&#160; &#160; Kath brings a wealth of experience to this role and I&#8217;m looking forward to working with her to develop new ideas and features that resonate with our audiences&#8217;.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>Kath Brown</strong> said: &#8220;The Telegraph is renowned for brilliant lifestyle content that inspires readers both in print and online. I am really excited about working with the team of talented experts who produce it.&#8221;</p>
