<div><p>Reps. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez and Ilhan Omar attracted an audience of nearly half a million people Tuesday night by <a href="https://www.twitch.tv/aoc" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">playing the viral video game Among Us on Twitch</a> &#8212; one of the biggest events in the history of the popular online streaming platform.</p>
<p>The freshmen Democrats organized the event as an impromptu get-out-the-vote effort aimed at young Americans, the latest in a series of video game events held by high-profile Democrats. Last week, former Vice President Joe Biden&#8217;s campaign also debuted an &#8220;island&#8221; on the popular game Animal Crossing.</p>
<p>Within an hour of AOC beginning <a href="https://www.twitch.tv/aoc" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">the stream</a> &#8212; her first on Twitch &#8212; close to 440,000 people were watching at the same time.</p>
<p>It was the third-largest audience ever for a live Twitch stream, <a href="https://www.axios.com/aoc-ilhan-omar-draw-400000-to-twitch-stream-to-get-out-the-vote-2ff44af0-b60a-4906-8cda-648ae65e1242.html">according to Axios</a>.</p>
<p>The &#8220;Squad&#8221; member&#8217;s profile on the platform already has nearly 500,000 subscribers, making her almost immediately one of the site&#8217;s most popular personalities.</p>
<p>Earlier this year, AOC made Twitch the centerpiece of her proposal to ban the US military from <a href="https://www.theverge.com/2020/7/30/21348451/military-recruiting-twitch-ban-block-amendment-ocasio-cortez">recruiting using online gaming platforms</a> &#8212; namely, disbanding the Army&#8217;s profile and recruitment presence on the site.</p>
