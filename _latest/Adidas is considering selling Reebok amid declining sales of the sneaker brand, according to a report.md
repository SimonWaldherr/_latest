<p>German sportswear brand Adidas is considering selling US sneaker company Reebok as early as March 2021, <a href="https://www.bloombergquint.com/onweb/adidas-is-preparing-sale-of-reebok-brand-manager-magazin-says">a source close to the matter told Bloomberg</a>.</p><p>Adidas has assigned an internal team, who have signed confidentiality agreements, to work on a potential deal, but this is in its early stages, the source said.</p><p>An Adidas spokesperson told Business Insider the company "does not comment on market rumors."</p><p>Adidas bought Reebok in 2006 for around 3.1 billion euros ($3.8 billion) &#8212; a bid to stay competitive with US sportswear rival Nike.</p>
