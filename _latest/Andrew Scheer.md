About Andrew Scheer

Andrew Scheer is the leader of the Conservative Party of Canada and leader of the Official Opposition. He was first elected as a Member of Parliament in 2004 in the riding of Regina-Qu'Appelle. He was elected Speaker in 2011 and held the post until the Liberals came to power in 2015. He was elected leader of the Conservatives in June of 2017.
