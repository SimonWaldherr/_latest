But be prepared to ante up some serious cash, as the starting price is $112,595.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">It will be&#160;powered by GM's proprietary&#160;Ultium batteries and&#160;Ultium Drive, GM&#8217;s proprietary EV drive units&#160;and&#160;have off-road capability.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p"><strong class="gnt_ar_b_al">Escalade:</strong><a href="https://www.usatoday.com/story/money/cars/2020/10/20/2021-cadillac-escalade-features-propel-luxury-suv-leadership/5988597002/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">2021 Cadillac Escalade's features and tech propel luxury SUV to leadership</a></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p"><strong class="gnt_ar_b_al">Recall:</strong><a href="https://www.usatoday.com/story/money/cars/2020/10/20/ford-recalls-ford-escape-transit-connect/5988640002/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Ford recalls 2020 Escape, Transit Connect to fix defects</a></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">&#8220;This innovative truck comes to market with speed and purpose, bringing GM another step closer to an all-electric future,&#8221; said Mark Reuss, GM's president. &#8220;With our Ultium Drive architecture as the foundation for an exceptionally broad range of performance, Hummer EV is perfect for the customer who wants capability, efficiency and performance.&#8221;</p><h2 class="gnt_ar_b_h2">Future trims</h2><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Every GMC Hummer EV Edition 1 will be identically appointed and fully equipped, GM said.
