<p><a href="https://drinksupercoffee.com/">Kitu Life</a> was born in Jordan DeCicco's college dorm room.</p><p>DeCicco was a basketball player looking for a more nutritious alternative to energy drinks. His older brothers, Jim and Jake, had also been college athletes and could feel his pain.</p><p>The siblings put their heads together and came up with <a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/super-coffee-review-shark-tank">Super Coffee</a>: a bottled coffee drink made with Colombian coffee, lactose-free protein, and MCT oil from coconuts.</p><p>Since then, Kitu Life (formerly known as Super Coffee) has made big strides.</p><p>In February 2018, the founders' appearance on "Shark Tank" aired on national TV. And while they didn't land a deal, they <a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/shark-tank-kitu-life-preparation-for-show-2018-11">previously told Business Insider</a> that the show gave them publicity and a chance to nail their pitch for future investor meetings.</p><p>In November of that year, after closing their series A round, they were valued at about $50 million. Today they're worth <a href="https://my.pitchbook.com/profile/125953-12/company/profile">$240 million</a>.</p><p>Kitu Life products &#8212; which include Super Coffee and Super Creamer &#8212; are sold in Wegmans, Whole Foods, and Fairway. Alex Rodriguez and Jennifer Lopez are investors, <a href="https://www.forbes.com/sites/igorbosilkovski/2020/09/25/jennifer-lopez-and-alex-rodriguez-invest-in-coffee-brand-super-coffee-cofounded-by-30-under-30-honorees/#1ffd56cd429a">Forbes reported</a>. And AB InBev, another investor, is distributing Super Coffee products in stores across the US, <a href="https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2020-06-30/startup-behind-super-coffee-gets-valuation-topping-200-million">Bloomberg reported</a>.</p><p>On "Shark Tank" and in subsequent pitch meetings, the Kitu Life founders used a 23-slide pitch deck that covers their backstory, what makes them special, and their financials. (Kitu Life said they tweaked the deck for each meeting.)</p>
