 <p>Lewellyn is facing charges of capital murder, rape, kidnapping and abuse of a corpse.</p> <p>Sutherland was reported missing after failing to return to the home she shared with her boyfriend Alex Nicholson.&#160;</p> <p>A search party was assembled to look for her.</p> <p>But two days later, Jackson County authorities said they had found her body buried in a deserted field alongside a rural highway near the city of Newport.</p> 



<p class="spacer"></p>
<img alt="">

 
<p class="credit"> (Image: Sydney Sutherland/Facebook)</p>

 <p>An Arkansas State Police investigator said in a probable cause affidavit that Lewellyn saw Sutherland jogging along the rural road, more than a mile from her home.</p> <p>During his interview with police, he allegedly admitted to ramming the young woman with his pickup, loading her into the truck, then driving her to a remote location, the affidavit said.</p> <p>Police allege Lewellyn then raped her and buried her at the same site.</p> 



<p class="spacer"></p>

 <p>The victim's phone was found 2.3 miles from where her body was discovered, police said in the affidavit.</p> <p>The investigators said they had seized Lewellyn's phone and used it to pinpoint his location in relation to where her body was found.</p> <p class="loading-circle primary-theme-spinner"></p>

<p>They allege it shows he had been in the vicinity of where the victim's body was buried, the affidavit continued.</p> <p>According to US reports, Sutherland and the suspect were known to each other but the nature of their relationship remains unclear.</p> <p>Llewellyn is scheduled to be arraigned on October 29.&#160;</p></div>
