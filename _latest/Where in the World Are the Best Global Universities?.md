<div><p>The best universities in the world are scattered in cities across the globe, from Stockholm to Beijing to Chicago; on four continents; and in 14 countries including Singapore, Germany and Saudi Arabia. </p><p>This week, U.S. News released the 2021 edition of its <a href="https://www.usnews.com/education/best-global-universities">Best Global Universities</a> rankings, which evaluate schools based on their academic research and reputation and allow students to compare them globally, regionally and within specific countries. Twenty-nine of the <a href="https://www.usnews.com/education/best-global-universities/rankings">top 50</a> universities are in North America, 13 in Europe, four in Asia and four in Australia/New Zealand.</p><p id="ad" class="ad lazy "></p><p>This year, however, more of the top 50 Best Global Universities are located outside of North America than in the previous edition of the rankings. The institutions in Europe that made the cut are spread across eight countries. In the U.K., six institutions appear in the top 50, with the <a href="https://www.usnews.com/education/best-global-universities/university-of-oxford-503637">University of Oxford,</a> ranked No. 5, leading the way. The other countries in Europe with universities ranked in the top 50 are Belgium, Denmark, France, Germany, the Netherlands, Sweden and Switzerland. </p><p>In Asia, two universities in Singapore, one in China and one in Saudi Arabia are included in the top 50. In Australia/New Zealand, four are located in Australia.</p><p id="ad" class="ad"></p><p>Unsurprisingly, many of the Best Global Universities are located in major metropolitan cities, like <a href="https://www.usnews.com/education/best-global-universities/sorbonne-universite-529569">Sorbonne Universite</a> in Paris and <a href="https://www.usnews.com/education/best-global-universities/university-college-london-501693">University College London</a>. Others are located in cities with smaller populations of less than 150,000, like <a href="https://www.usnews.com/education/best-global-universities/cornell-university-190415">Cornell University</a> in Ithaca, New York, and the <a href="https://www.usnews.com/education/best-global-universities/university-of-michigan-ann-arbor-170976">University of Michigan&#8212;Ann Arbor</a>.</p><p>Many of these institutions are also ranked among the best universities in their region. The <a href="https://www.usnews.com/education/best-global-universities/university-of-melbourne-501796">University of Melbourne,</a> for example, is ranked No. 1 in Australia/New Zealand and No. 25 overall, and <a href="https://www.usnews.com/education/best-global-universities/tsinghua-university-503146">Tsinghua University</a> in Beijing is ranked No. 1 in Asia and No. 28 overall. </p><p>International students and their families can use the map below to explore highly ranked global universities in or near their home country as well as those located farther away.</p><p></p><p id="ad" class="ad lazy "></p><p><i>See the <a href="https://www.usnews.com/education/best-global-universities">complete rankings</a> of the Best Global Universities.</i></p></div>
