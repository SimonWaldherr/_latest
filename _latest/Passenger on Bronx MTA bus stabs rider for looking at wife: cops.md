<div><p>A passenger on a Bronx MTA bus flew into a rage and repeatedly stabbed another man whom he accused of looking at his wife, according to police.</p>
<p>The 31-year-old victim and the alleged attacker &#8212; along with his spouse &#8212; were at a Bx41 bus stop on Willis Avenue near East 149th Street in Mott Haven around 6:10 p.m. Oct. 6 when the suspect made the accusation, authorities said.</p>
<p>All three got on the bus, where the squabble turned violent, police said.</p>
<p>The suspect brandished a knife and stabbed the victim numerous times, drawing blood, cops said.</p>
<p>He was taken to Lincoln Medical Center in stable condition.</p>
<p>The suspect &#8212; shown in a surveillance photo released by cops wearing glasses, a red shirt and pants and a black jacket &#8212; took off, heading south on Third Avenue.</p>
