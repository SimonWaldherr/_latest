<p>Yolanda Hadid cannot get enough of her &#8220;angel&#8221; granddaughter.</p>
<p>On Sunday, the former &#8220;Real Housewives of Beverly Hills&#8221; star, 56, shared a sweet photo of daughter Gigi Hadid and Zayn Malik&#8217;s baby girl, as the infant held Yolanda&#8217;s hand.</p>
<p>&#8220;My heart is expending with so much love and joy for this little baby girl she is an angel sent to us from above&#8230;..&#8221; Yolanda captioned the post on Instagram.</p>
<p>Yolanda, who was born in the Netherlands, also referenced herself as &#8220;oma,&#8221; which means &#8220;grandma&#8221; in Dutch.</p>
<p>&#8220;Thank you Mommy &amp; Daddy for making me a Oma, I love every minute of it,&#8221; she continued.</p>
<p>Last month, Malik, 27, announced that Gigi, 25, <a href="https://pagesix.com/2020/09/23/zayn-malik-confirms-birth-of-first-child-with-gigi-hadid-on-twitter/">had given birth</a> to the couple&#8217;s first child, revealing their baby girl is &#8220;healthy &amp; beautiful.&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;The love i feel for this tiny human is beyond my understanding. Grateful to know her, proud to call her mine, &amp; thankful for the life we will have together,&#8221; <a href="https://twitter.com/zaynmalik/status/1308947122126032896" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">the proud dad tweeted</a>.</p>
<p>Gigi, <a href="https://pagesix.com/article/pregnant-celebrities-this-year/">who confirmed her pregnancy</a> in April, <a href="https://pagesix.com/2020/09/24/gigi-hadid-is-so-in-love-with-her-and-zayn-maliks-baby-girl/">added on Instagram</a> that she and Malik are &#8220;so in love&#8221; with their newborn daughter.</p>
<p>&#8220;Our girl joined us earth-side this weekend and she&#8217;s already changed our world,&#8221; the supermodel wrote at the time.</p>
<p><a href="https://pagesix.com/2020/10/01/gigi-hadid-wears-mama-necklace-after-giving-birth-to-baby-girl-with-zayn-malik/">Since welcoming their baby girl</a> into the world &#8212; Gigi is <a href="https://pagesix.com/2020/09/26/gigi-hadid-gave-birth-to-newborn-daughter-on-a-farm-source/">rumored to have given birth</a> at a Pennsylvania farm &#8212; both mom and dad have been showered with well-wishes and gifts from their celebrity friends, <a href="https://pagesix.com/2020/10/16/tan-france-gifts-gigi-hadid-and-zayn-maliks-baby-zigis-girl-onesies/">including a &#8220;Zigi Girl&#8221; onesie</a> from &#8220;Queer Eye&#8217;s&#8221; Tan France.</p>
<p>In addition to Gigi, Yolanda shares daughter Bella, 24, and son Anwar, 21, with ex Mohamed Hadid. She was previously married to David Foster.</p>
