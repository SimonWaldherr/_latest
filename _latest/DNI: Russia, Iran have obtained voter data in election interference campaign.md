<div><p>DNI: Russia, Iran have obtained voter data in election interference campaign The DNI made the announcement Wednesday evening.</p><p id="_ap_link_national security_NSA_">Senior <a href="https://abcnews.go.com/alerts/nsa" id="_ap_link_national security_NSA_" target="_blank">national security</a> officials alerted the American public Wednesday that <a href="https://abcnews.go.com/alerts/iran" id="_ap_link_Iran_Iran_" target="_blank">Iran</a> and <a href="https://abcnews.go.com/alerts/russia-nation" id="_ap_link_Russia_RussiaNation_" target="_blank">Russia</a> have both obtained voter data in their efforts to interfere in the 2020 U.S. <a href="https://abcnews.go.com/alerts/elections" id="_ap_link_election_Elections_" target="_blank">election</a>.</p><p>"This data can be used by foreign actors to attempt to communicate false information to registered voters that they hope will cause confusion, so chaos, and undermine your confidence in American democracy," Director of National Intelligence John Ratcliffe said in a surprise news conference Wednesday evening.</p><p class="Ad incontentTeads incontentTeads--story ad-slot"></p><p>Ratcliffe also announce that Iran was separately behind a series of threatening emails that were found to be sent this week to Democratic voters.</p><p>Florida law enforcement and the FBI previously had said they were <a href="https://abcnews.go.com/Politics/florida-sheriffs-fbi-investigating-emails-threatening-voters/story?id=73740479" target="_blank">investigating the threatening emails</a> allegedly sent from outside the United States to registered Democrats. The emails claimed to be from by a member of the Proud Boys, according to authorities, something the group denies.</p><p>According to sheriffs' offices in both Brevard County and Alachua County, the sender claimed to belong to the "alt-right" group and said they were in possession of a voter's personal information.</p><p>The Brevard County Sheriff's Office said the email addressed the voter by name, then stated: "We are in possession of all your information. You are currently registered as a Democrat and we know this because we have gained access into the entire voting infrastructure. You will vote for Trump on Election Day or we will come after you."</p><p>The Proud Boys denied any involvement in the incident.</p><p>This is a developing news story. Please check back for updates.</p></div>
