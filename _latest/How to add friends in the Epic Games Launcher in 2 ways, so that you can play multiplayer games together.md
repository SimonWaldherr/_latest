<p><a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/category/epic-games" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Epic Games</a> makes "Fortnite" &#8212; one of the most popular multiplayer games in the world. There are hundreds of other multiplayer titles on the Epic Games store, meaning they're best played with others.</p><p>Epic Games lets you add other users to a Friends List, which lets you message them, invite them to games, and quickly squad up when you're ready to play.</p><p>You can find friends by searching for their Epic Games display name or the email address linked to their account. You can also connect your Facebook or Steam profile to find out which of your friends there also have a connected Epic Games account.</p><h2><strong>How to add friends on Epic Games</strong></h2><p>You can add friends in the Epic Games Launcher by searching for their display name or email address.</p>
<p id="gpt-post-sai-mobile_in_post_ad-fluid-2" class="ad ad-wrapper fluid in-post only-mobile"></p>

<p><strong>1. </strong>Open the Epic Games Launcher.</p><p><strong>2. </strong>Click the "Friends" icon on the left hand side. A pop-up window will appear.</p><p>

</p><p><strong>5. </strong>To view pending requests, click the "Requests" tab on the far-right of this window.</p>

<p id="gpt-post-sai-desktop_in_post_ad-fluid-3" class="ad ad-wrapper fluid in-post only-desktop"></p>
<p>You can also add friends by connecting a Facebook or Steam account.</p><p><strong>6 </strong>Underneath the field where you can enter a display name or email, click "Connections."</p><p><strong>7. </strong>You'll see an icon for Steam and for Facebook. Clicking either one will open a web browser, where you can connect your accounts. If either one has a chain link icon in the corner, that means it's already connected.</p><p>If you're trying to connect a Steam account, you'll also probably have to enter a code that's sent to your email.</p><p>

</p><p><strong>8. </strong>Once you've connected your account, click on the "Suggestions" tab. There you'll see a list of suggested friends.&#160;</p>
