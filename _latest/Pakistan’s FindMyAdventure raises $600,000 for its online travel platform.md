<div><p>Karachi based travel platform, FindMyAdventure has raised $600,000 in a pre-series A round from a local Pakistani and expatriate consortium. Where COVID-19 financially hit many companies badly, for FindMyAdventure their business gained overwhelming response. In a statement issued they said: &#8220;The team worked around the clock to develop contingency plans and became one of the first companies to expand into digital tourism and experiences within Pakistan.&#8221;</p>


<p>The platform was founded in 2016 by four young Pakistanis as an online platform providing users to book travel experiences around Pakistan. The platform has now expanded into rentals, luxury tourism, and digital tourism experiences.</p>

<p>It is pertinent to note that FindMyAdventure currently has over 300 online/offline travel operators on its platform and claims to serve over 30,000 customers across the country.</p>

<p>Founder and CEO, Komail Naqvi, said: &#8220;The faith which expatriate angels have shown in Pakistani tourism and us, is what has brought FMA here. Without their support, and the dedication of our team, the business would not have survived this hit.&#8221;</p>

<img alt="" class="image photo" src="https://sm.mashable.com/mashable_pk/photo/default/img-20201019-131853_5g83.jpg">

<p>The startup in a statement said that their sales have skyrocketed since it resumed operations. &#8220;Although things weren&#8217;t where they were supposed to be by this time of the year, they were certainly getting there,&#8221; the statement reads.</p>
