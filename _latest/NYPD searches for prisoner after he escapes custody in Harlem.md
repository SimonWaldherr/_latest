<div><p>A cuffed inmate pushed a cop while being transported and took off into Harlem Thursday morning, police said.</p>
<p>The 25-year-old man had just been discharged from the hospital, and officers were taking him to the precinct to be processed around 9:50 a.m. when he ran off near Lenox Avenue And W. 137 Street, according to cops.</p>
<p>Police were canvassing the neighborhood.</p>
<p>&#8220;He&#8217;s cuffed so he shouldn&#8217;t be that hard to find,&#8221; a police spokesman said.</p>
<p>The man was in custody for violating an order of protection, cops said.</p>
