<p>The talk show host, 62, tried out a new slicked-back hair look on her namesake talk show this week, debuting a pompadour style to go with her signature blazer and button-up.</p>
<p>Some fans complimented the new &#8216;do <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/CGk6dgBBFsr/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">on Instagram</a>, with one remarking that DeGeneres&#8217; style resembled her wife Portia de Rossi&#8217;s similar blond cut.</p>
<p>The hair change comes amid a tumultuous time for the TV host, who <a href="https://pagesix.com/2020/09/21/ellen-degeneres-returns-to-tv-addresses-toxic-workplace-allegations/">opened her show&#8217;s new season with a mea culpa monologue</a>&#160;addressing harassment claims from former employees. She apologized for the toxic workplace allegations, but also threw in some jokes that <a href="https://pagesix.com/2020/09/22/ellen-degeneres-apology-monologue-panned-by-former-employees/">angered the accusers</a>.</p>
<p>Seems the star is making efforts to change, both inside and out.</p>
