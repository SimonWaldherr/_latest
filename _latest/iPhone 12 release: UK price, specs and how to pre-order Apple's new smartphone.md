<div><p>Apple has <a href="https://www.telegraph.co.uk/technology/2020/10/13/iphone-12-launch-live-apple-event-2020-keynote-updates-release/">unveiled four new iPhones</a>, all of which will be able to connect to 5G networks across the UK.</p>
<p>The company is hoping that cheaper iPhone 12 Mini will attract customers at its &#163;699 price, while others may opt for the standard iPhone 12. And then at the top of the market is the iPhone 12 Pro and iPhone 12 Pro Max with advanced new cameras.</p>
<h3>When will the iPhone 12 be released?</h3>
<p>Apple has two different release dates for its new line-up of iPhones. The iPhone 12 and iPhone 12 Pro will be available to pre-order from October 16 and will be released on October 23.</p>
<p>The iPhone 12 Mini and iPhone 12 Pro Max will be available to pre-order from November 6 and will be released on November 13.</p>
