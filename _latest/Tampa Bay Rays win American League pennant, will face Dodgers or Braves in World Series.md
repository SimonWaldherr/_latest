<div><p class="endmarkEnabled">The Tampa Bay Rays won the American League pennant on Saturday, guaranteeing the 2020 World Series champ will be first-time winner of this century.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled"><a href="https://www.mlb.com/news/randy-arozarena-2020-alcs-record-performance" target="_blank">Randy Arozarena slugged a two-run homer</a> and <a href="https://www.mlb.com/player/charlie-morton-450203" target="_blank">starting pitcher Charlie Morton </a>shut down his former teammates, leading Tampa Bay to a 4-2 victory over the <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/news/sports/houston-astros-gm-manager-suspended-over-theft-pitching-signs-n1114701" target="_blank">scandal-plagued Houston Astros</a> in Game 7 of the AL Championship Series in San Diego.</p><p></p><p id="taboolaReadMoreBelow"></p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Arozarena hit his first-inning shot off losing Houston pitcher Lance McCullers Jr., an alum of Tampa's Jesuit High School, which is a mere 20 miles from the Rays' home park, Tropicana Field in St. Petersburg.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Morton, who won a World Series ring pitching for the Astros in 2017, threw 5 2/3 shutout innings on Saturday.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">The Rays will face the National League Championship Series winner, either the Atlanta Braves or Los Angeles Dodgers. The <a href="https://mlb.nbcsports.com/2020/10/17/dodgers-force-nlcs-game-7-vs-braves-with-3-1-win/" target="_blank">deciding Game 7 of the NLCS </a>is set <a href="https://www.nbclosangeles.com/news/sports/dodgers-braves-nlcs-game-six/2445564/" target="_blank">for Sunday</a>, with first pitch at 8:15 p.m. ET.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">The <a href="https://www.mlb.com/postseason" target="_blank">neutral-site World Series starts Tuesday</a> at <a href="https://www.mlb.com/rangers/ballpark" target="_blank">Globe Life Field</a>, the <a href="https://www.nbcdfw.com/news/sports/red-fever/world-series-to-be-held-in-arlington-first-time-at-one-site-since-1944-ap/2444257/" target="_blank">new home of the Texas Rangers, in Arlington</a>.</p><p></p><p class="endmarkEnabled">"I'm extremely grateful. This hasn't been the easiest road to this point," said Rays catcher Mike Zunino, who also hit a home run Saturday.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">"My wife's been amazing, she's supported me through this whole thing, I love you babe. But I mean this is what we play for. It's going to be fun. We're ready to head to Dallas."</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">The Astros were trying to cheat history and become only the second team in baseball history to rally from a 3-0 deficit in a best-of-7 playoff series.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">"These guys fought, I mean they fought to the very end," Astros Manager Dusty Baker said. "This team is a bunch of fighters, a bunch of guys with a tremendous amount of perseverance and fortitude."</p><p></p><p class="endmarkEnabled">The ALCS was played at San Diego's Petco Park as MLB hopes <a href="https://www.nbcsports.com/bayarea/giants/mlb-playoffs-be-held-bubble-four-locations-texas-california#:~:text=MLB%20and%20the%20Players%20Association,division%20series%20for%20both%20leagues.&amp;text=The%20Athletic's%20Ken%20Rosenthal%20first,of%20the%20first%2Dround%20series." target="_blank">bubbles will keep players and staff safe</a> from the <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/health/coronavirus" target="_blank">coronavirus pandemic</a>. The virus forced much of <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/news/sports/what-we-know-about-mlb-s-plans-baseball-season-n1156821" target="_blank">spring training to be canceled </a>and MLB teams played a <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/it-s-whole-new-ballgame-america-s-pastime-will-have-n1231904" target="_blank">truncated 60-game schedule</a>, far less than the normal, annual slate of 162 games.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Tampa Bay's 2020 AL title is no fluke, as the Rays were the AL's best team in the regular season, <a href="https://www.mlb.com/standings" target="_blank">winning 40 of those 60 games</a>.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">With the Astros, controversial world champions of 2017, now out of the picture, that means the next World Series winner will bring home a trophy not seen in their city for decades - and in the Rays' case, ever.</p><ul class=""><li>Tampa Bay, an expansion team that began play in 1998, won the 2008 AL pennant before falling to the <a href="https://www.nbcphiladelphia.com/news/sports/phillies/2008_world_series_champion_phillies__where_are_they_now_/2028148/" target="_blank">Philadelphia Phillies in the World Series</a>. This marks their first trip back to the Fall Classic since.</li><li>Atlanta had been one of baseball&#8217;s model franchises of the 1990s and early 2000s with the &#8220;Big Three&#8221; starting pitchers of John Smoltz, Greg Maddux and Tom Glavine. But the Braves consistently fell short in October, and <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=etf9rcbhfTM" target="_blank">Atlanta captured its only World Series title in 1995 </a>by besting the Cleveland Indians in six games.</li><li>Meanwhile, the Dodgers <a href="https://www.mlb.com/news/longest-world-series-title-droughts" target="_blank">have baseball&#8217;s 10th-longest title-less string</a>, in a remarkable run of October futility given L.A.&#8217;s recent run of April-through-September domination. The Dodgers, champions of the NL West for eight straight seasons, haven&#8217;t won the World Series since 1988, when <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/nightly-news/video/andrea-mitchell-on-president-reagans-last-day-in-office-39619139997" target="_blank">Ronald Reagan occupied the White House </a> and <a href="https://www.nbcbayarea.com/news/business/six-years-after-last-tweet-blockbuster-is-making-a-comeback/2342799/" target="_blank">Blockbuster was still</a> years away from being the dominant force in home entertainment.</li></ul><p></p><p class="endmarkEnabled">The bridesmaid Dodgers have also run into bad luck, losing the 2017 and 2018 World Series to the Astros <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/news/sports/red-sox-part-ways-alex-cora-amid-astros-sign-stealing-n1115711" target="_blank">and Boston Red Sox</a>,both<a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/l-city-council-asks-mlb-name-dodgers-world-champs-amid-n1119746" target="_blank"> later implicated in sign-stealing scandals</a>.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Since 2000, the <a href="https://www.mlb.com/news/world-series-trophy-profile/c-155729034" target="_blank">Commissioner's Trophy</a> has been lifted four times by <a href="https://www.nbcboston.com/news/local/were_2018_red_sox_best_team_of_the_decade__mlb_com_reveals_its_no__1/2001790/" target="_blank">the Red Sox</a> (2004, 2007, 2013, 2018), three times by the <a href="https://www.nbcbayarea.com/news/sports/top-5-san-francisco-giants-postseason-moments-of-2014/78515/" target="_blank">San Francisco Giants</a> (2010, 2012, 2014), twice by <a href="https://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/local/its-ours-yankees-capture-27th-world-series-title/1875972/" target="_blank">the New York Yankees</a> (2000, 2009) and St. Louis Cardinals (2006, 2011), and once each by the Phillies, <a href="https://www.nbcsports.com/looking-back-2001-world-series" target="_blank">Arizona Diamondbacks</a> (2001), <a href="https://www.nbcchicago.com/news/sports/chicago-baseball/inside-the-crowd-at-wrigley-field-after-cubs_-world-series-win_chicago/90837/" target="_blank">Chicago Cubs </a>(2016), <a href="https://www.nbclosangeles.com/news/local/astros-dodgers-world-series-game-seven/26638/" target="_blank">Houston Astros </a>(2017), <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=e2uUmGCMo-c" target="_blank">Florida Marlins</a> (2003), <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MOOe78Kdxrc" target="_blank">Anaheim Angels</a> (2002), <a href="https://www.nbcbayarea.com/news/sports/celebrities-react-kansas-city-royals-world-series-win/109839/" target="_blank">Kansas City Royals</a> (2015), <a href="https://www.nbcsports.com/chicago/white-sox/white-sox-2005-rewind-15-best-moments-world-series-run" target="_blank">Chicago White Sox</a> (2005) and last year's champion, the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdFS3g659uA" target="_blank">Washington Nationals</a>.</p></div>
