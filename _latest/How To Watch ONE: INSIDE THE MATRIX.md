<div><p>How To Watch ONE: INSIDE THE MATRIX</p><p>ONE Championship is returning to &#8220;The Lion City&#8221; with its biggest show of the year on Friday, 30 October.</p>
<p>The promotion&#8217;s upcoming blockbuster, <a href="https://www.onefc.com/events/inside-the-matrix/">ONE: INSIDE THE MATRIX</a>, will bring fans across the globe four massive World Title matchups live from the Singapore Indoor Stadium.</p>
<p>In the main event, two-division ONE World Champion <a href="https://www.onefc.com/athletes/aung-la-n-sang/">Aung La &#8220;The Burmese Python&#8221; N Sang</a> defends his middleweight strap against undefeated submission specialist <a href="https://www.onefc.com/athletes/reinier-de-ridder/">Reinier &#8220;The Dutch Knight&#8221; De Ridder</a>.</p>
<p>Also, in the co-main event, ONE Lightweight World Champion <a href="https://www.onefc.com/athletes/christian-lee/">Christian &#8220;The Warrior&#8221; Lee</a> puts his gold on the line against another undefeated juggernaut in <a href="https://www.onefc.com/athletes/iuri-lapicus/">Iuri Lapicus</a>.</p>
<p>The card also features featherweight king <a href="https://www.onefc.com/athletes/martin-nguyen/">Martin &#8220;The Situ-Asian&#8221; Nguyen</a> defending his belt against the always-exciting <a href="https://www.onefc.com/athletes/thanh-le/">Thanh Le</a>.</p>
<p>Plus, <a href="https://www.onefc.com/athletes/xiong-jing-nan/">&#8220;The Panda&#8221; Xiong Jing Nan</a>&#160;and <a href="https://www.onefc.com/athletes/tiffany-teo/">Tiffany &#8220;No Chill&#8221; Teo</a> will fight for the Chinese star&#8217;s ONE Women&#8217;s Strawweight World Championship in a rematch of their 2018 classic.</p>
<p>Along with those World Title fights, two other high-profile bouts round out the stacked card for what&#8217;s sure to be an unforgettable night in The Home Of Martial Arts.</p>

<p>Additionally, the first two bouts will be streamed on ONE&#8217;s <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ONEChampionship/">Facebook</a> and <a href="https://twitter.com/ONEChampionship">Twitter</a> pages.</p>
