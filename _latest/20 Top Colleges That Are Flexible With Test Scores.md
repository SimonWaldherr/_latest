University of Chicago

School rank and category: 6 (tie), National Universities

Test submission policy: The University of Chicago doesn't require standardized test scores. While UChicago is test-optional, the school will consider submitted SAT and ACT scores as part of its admissions process, according to the college's website. International students must prove English language proficiency by providing TOEFL, IELTS or Duolingo scores, though some exemptions are made for students who had English as the primary language of schooling and communication or who spent at least one academic year full time in a qualified postsecondary school where English was the language of instruction.
