<div><p class="sc-pHJJA iocNwz above-article-prompt-donations"></p><p><a href="/topic/thailand">Thailand</a> has welcomed its first international travellers in seven months as part of a new &#8220;Special Tourist Visa&#8221; programme.</p><p>The 39 <a href="/topic/tourists">tourists</a>, who flew into Bangkok from Shanghai, will need to stay in the country for a minimum of 30 days under the rules of the special initiative, which was approved by the Thai cabinet in September.</p><p></p><p>As part of the requirements for the visa, the visitors must take out special insurance policies, get a Covid-19 test before they travel and prove they have accommodation for the duration of their stay in the country.</p><p id="article-im-prompt"></p><p>Once they get to Thailand, they must stay in a government-approved hotel or hospital and quarantine for the first 14 days. They will also need to download the official <a href="/topic/coronavirus">coronavirus</a>-tracking app and use it during their time in the country.</p><p></p><p></p><p>Only those travelling from Thailand&#8217;s list of low-risk countries can apply for the Special Tourist Visa. The visas are valid for up to 90 days, and can be renewed twice.</p><p>While all international flights will arrive into Bangkok for the time-being, future destinations may include Phuket if the programme is successful.</p><p id="taboola-carousel-thumbnails"></p><p>Thailand&#8217;s tourism ministry is expecting two other flights from China later this year, as well as around 400 foreign visitors in early November, the <i>Associated Press</i> reported.</p><p>At the moment, Thailand is on the UK Foreign Office&#8217;s safe list, and visitors don&#8217;t need to self-isolate on return.</p><p>However, the UK is currently not on Thailand&#8217;s list of low-risk countries, which means those travelling from the UK won&#8217;t be able to apply for the visa.</p><p>Thailand&#8217;s borders are only open to travellers from the UK in certain situations, such as if you hold a valid work permit, or are an international student.</p><p class="sc-ptCms fzKffT"></p></div>
