title: > 
  Adama Traore 'ready to sign new £100,000-a-week deal with Wolves' 
description: > 
  Adama Traore's impressive form is set to bring with it fresh reward with the Wolves winger reportedly set to be handed a new £100,000-a-week deal.Traore, who made his international debut for Spain this month, has generated interest from Liverpool and Barcelona but is now expected to remain with Wolves on bumper new terms.'You can see how happy he's been in training just by seeing us be together again,' boss Nuno Espirito Santo said, ahead of Monday's trip to face Leeds.He has developed impressively under Espirito Santo and has now been recognised by Spain boss Luis Enrique.'I was delighted and really happy for Adama because he made his debut and he got a start which shows the progress and the impact he's had,' Espirito Santo added.
