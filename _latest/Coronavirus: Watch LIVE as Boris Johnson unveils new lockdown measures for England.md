Boris Johnson is set to unveil the latest coronavirus lockdown plans for England.

The Prime Minister will address parliament about a new three-tier system to try and stem the second wave of the deadly bug.

It comes as England reported 36 new deaths in its hospitals, Wales had four and Northern Ireland recorded three, with no new deaths in Scotland.

The grim figure is the highest increase on a Monday since 62 deaths were confirmed on June 8.

Earlier today it was announced that three temporary Nightingale hospitals in the north of England could be brought back into use to deal with the spike in coronavirus cases.
