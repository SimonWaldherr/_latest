<div><p>Pakistani artists and TikTok stars recently participated in an online rap video relay conducted by Infinix, a smartphone company. The participants were able to score a Guinness World Record for the &#8220;most people in an online rapping video relay&#8221;. A major feat indeed.</p>

<img alt="" class="image photo" src="https://sm.mashable.com/mashable_pk/photo/default/pic-1603177114_w9qx.jpg">

<p>The video relay included 51 participants from 15 different countries, among which 5 were from Pakistan. The participants included YouTuber Asif Bali, the TikTok star Lerry Khan who has over a million followers, and fellow TikTokers Hassan, Moaz, and Taimoor. Infinix Pakistan also had their own representative from Pakistan, ZuofyshanAhmer, the community Executive, who enthusiastically participated in the relay.</p>

<img alt="" class="image photo" src="https://sm.mashable.com/mashable_pk/photo/default/pic-1603177027_xug2.jpg">

<p> </p>

<p>The video was broadcasted live on the internet as the participants rapped in Urdu, Chinese, Arabic, Nigerian, Hindin and other languages. The record attempt was conducted on September 30 and lasted for a whopping 1 hour and 11 minutes. The video relay was made to celebrate the launch of Infinix&#8217;s latest state of the art device, the Infinix Hot 10.</p>
