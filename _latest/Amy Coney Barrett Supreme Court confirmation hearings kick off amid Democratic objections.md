Barrett plans to tell the Senate that she is “forever grateful” for Ginsburg’s trailblazing path as a woman. But she is resolved to maintain the perspective of her own mentor, the late conservative Justice Antonin Scalia and “apply the law as written,” according to her remarks for the hearings, which start as the country battles to fight off COVID-19.
