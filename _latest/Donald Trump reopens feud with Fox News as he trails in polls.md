<div><p class="mol-para-with-font">President <a href="/news/donald_trump/index.html" id="mol-06c4ef20-1479-11eb-b811-950d8255072a">Donald Trump</a> renewed his feud with Fox News' polls after they showed him trailing Democratic rival <a href="/news/joe-biden/index.html" id="mol-06c675c0-1479-11eb-b811-950d8255072a">Joe Biden</a> in a few key battleground states.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">The latest round of polling from Fox showed President Trump leading in Ohio but trailing Biden closely in Pennsylvania and Wisconsin while losing big in Michigan.&#160;</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">All states are crucial toward the 270 electoral votes a candidate needs to win the White House.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">Biden leads by 12 points in Michigan (52-40 per cent), 5 points in Pennsylvania (50-45 per cent), and 5 points in Wisconsin (49-44 per cent). Trump is up in Ohio by 3 points (48-45 per cent), according to the latest <a class="class" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener" target="_blank" href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/fox-news-poll-trump-gains-in-ohio-biden-ahead-in-michigan-pennsylvania-wisconsin">Fox News polls</a>.&#160;</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">The president took to Twitter to express his fury at the numbers, blasting them as 'fake' and claiming he leads in all those states.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">'.@FoxNews&#160;Polls are totally FAKE, just like they were in 2016. I am leading in all of the states mentioned, which you will soon see. I thought Fox was getting rid of its pollster. Sadly, it never happened!,' he wrote.</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">And Trump doesn't lead in all those states. href="https://www.realclearpolitics.com/elections/trump-vs-biden-top-battleground-states/">RealClearPolitics average</a> of polls of those states.&#160;</p><p class="mol-para-with-font">Michigan,
