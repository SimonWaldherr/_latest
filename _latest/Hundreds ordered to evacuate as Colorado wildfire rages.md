Other areas were also under new evacuation orders.</p><p></p><p id="taboolaReadMoreBelow"></p><p class="endmarkEnabled">The blaze also closed western areas of Rocky Mountain National Park.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">No injuries have been reported.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">The fire was impacting the Grand Lake area and the Highway 34 corridor, fire officials said. Firefighters were working to contain the fire on the west side of highway, the Grand County Office of Emergency Management said.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">There are several large wildfires burning in Colorado, including the largest in recorded state history, <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/record-setting-cameron-peak-wildfire-consumes-more-200-000-acres-n1243897" target="_blank">the Cameron Peak Fire</a> in the mountains west of Fort Collins.</p><p class="lazyload-placeholder"></p><p class="caption__container">Smoke obscures the sun as fire approaches a ridge along Highway 36 as several wildfires burn in the state Wednesday, Oct. 21, 2020, south of Lyons, Colo.</p><p class="caption__source">David Zalubowski / AP</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">"The safety of the communities being impacted and our firefighters remains our highest priority," a statement on an interagency web site, InciWeb, said in a statement Wednesday night of the East Troublesome Fire and the new threats there.</p><p></p><p class="endmarkEnabled">The blaze was estimated to be about 30,000 acres Wednesday night, fire incident commander Jay Winfield said in a video briefing.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">High winds and smoke grounded heavy helicopter and air tankers that had been flying Wednesday morning, he said. The fire is 10 percent contained, fire officials said earlier.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">"As we began today we were hovering right around that 19,000 acreage mark, so significant growth today," Winfield said Wednesday evening.</p><blockquote class="twitter-tweet"><p lang="en" dir="ltr">View from the house in Granby.
