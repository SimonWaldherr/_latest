<div><p>Anti-vaxxers are spreading antisemitic conspiracy theories about coronavirus, a report by the Government's independent adviser on anti-semitism has found.</p>
<p>The research discovered anti-vaxxers on both the right and left are spewing online hate linking the pandemic to &#8220;Jews plotting to take over the world."</p>
<p>Authors Lord Mann, Boris Johnson's antisemitism tsar and molecular biologist Dr Lewis Arthurton, studied 28 of the most popular <a href="https://www.telegraph.co.uk/health-fitness/mind/race-covid-vaccine-anti-vax-background-noise-getting-louder/">antivaxxer forums on social media</a> - including one run by Jeremy Corbyn's brother Piers - and found all of them had shared anti-semitic posts. Overall, the report found antisemitism present in 79 per cent of antivaxxer networks.&#160;</p>
<p>&#8220;Covid-19 conspiracists attributed the &#8216;hoax&#8217; to the &#8216;global elite&#8217; naming the Rothschilds, the Rockefellers, George Soros, &#8216;Zionists&#8217;, as well as Bill Gates,&#8221; said the report.</p>
