<div><p>Click to email this to a friend (Opens in new window)</p><p>Click to share on Twitter (Opens in new window)</p><p>Click to share on Facebook (Opens in new window)</p><p>Kylie Jenner has reached a settlement in the lawsuit over her lingerie designs.</p>
<p>Back in January, the 23-year-old beauty mogul <a href="https://pagesix.com/2020/01/16/kendall-and-kylie-jenner-sued-over-lacy-lingerie/">was sued by Klauber Bros, Inc.</a>, a company that creates original lace designs and claimed that Jenner had used one of its copyrighted styles on the waistband of a thong sold on her <a href="https://kyliejennershop.com/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer">Kylie Shop</a>.</p>
<p>Now, after initially seeking all profits from sales of the style &#8212; which is no longer listed on the star&#8217;s website &#8212; Klauber Bros is dismissing all claims against Jenner, with the suit settled for an undisclosed sum.</p>
<p>&#8220;PLEASE TAKE NOTICE that Plaintiff Klauber Brothers, Inc. has reached a settlement agreement as to the claims against Defendants The Kylie Shop, Inc. and Kylie Jenner, Inc.,&#8221; read the court documents dated October 12.</p>
<p>The Blast <a href="https://theblast.com/145651/kylie-jenner-settles-lawsuit-over-lingerie" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">was the first to report</a> the settlement news.</p>
