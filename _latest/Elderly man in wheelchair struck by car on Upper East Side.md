<div><p>A driver plowed into a 94-year-old man in a wheelchair on the Upper East Side Tuesday afternoon, leaving the elderly victim in critical but stable condition, police said.</p>
<p>The victim was crossing 5th Avenue west to east in a motorized wheelchair around 3 p.m. when the 32-year-old driver, who was heading south on 5th Avenue in a Toyota Camry, hit him, cops said.</p>
<p>It was not immediately clear who had the right of way, cops said.</p>
<p>The unidentified victim suffered cuts to the head and is being treated at NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital, according to authorities.</p>
<p>The driver, who was also not identified, stayed on the scene, and is not being charged with a crime, cops said.</p>
