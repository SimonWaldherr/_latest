<div><p>A Louisiana man brought his alleged 11-year-old rape victim to the hospital, only to lead police on a chase when doctors learned the girl was pregnant with his child.</p>
<p>Wendell Sanchez, 34, dropped off the child at the emergency room and waited in his vehicle after she complained of stomach pains, according to police.</p>
<p>Staff alerted cops that the girl was pregnant and Sanchez was believed to be the father.</p>
<p>When deputies arrived, Sanchez fled the scene, abandoning his car and running on foot to a nearby apartment complex.</p>
<p>Police found him hiding on a patio, where they say he was in possession of illegal narcotics, marijuana and drug paraphernalia.</p>
<p>Sanchez faces a slew of felony charges, including first degree rape with a victim under the age of 13 and the creation of a clandestine lab.</p>
<p>His bond is set at more than a half million dollars.</p>
