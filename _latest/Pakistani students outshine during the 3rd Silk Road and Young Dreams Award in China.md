<div><p>Pakistani students won prizes in the speech, photography, music, singing and sand painting competitions during the annual 3rd Silk Road and Young Dreams Award ceremony held in Beijing, China.</p>

<p>This year, all the competitions were held online in the wake of COVID-19 under the theme of Health amidst this pandemic.</p>

<p>In the speech competition, Danish Yaqoob from Pakistan bagged the first prize while Abdul Rehman, a student from PECB secured third position.</p>

<p>Maaz Ali Nadeem, a student of PECB, won the most popular award in photography competition while Areeba Ali and Thembekile, students from PECB, secured the excellence of music and speech contest awards.</p>

<p>Pakistan Embassy in Beijing's Deputy Head of Mission, Ahmed Farooq distributed prizes among the winners. Muhammad Ali Asad from Pakistan Embassy College Beijing (PECB) served as the judge of speech competition.</p>

<img alt="" class="image photo" src="https://sm.mashable.com/mashable_pk/photo/default/img-20201019-124748_f4v9.jpg">

<p> </p>

<p>Silk Road Cities Alliance, China Friendship Foundation for Peace and Development, Beijing Belt and Road Cooperative Community and Chongyang Institute for Financial Studies of Renmin University jointly hosted the ceremony that was attended by participants from over 30 countries.</p>
