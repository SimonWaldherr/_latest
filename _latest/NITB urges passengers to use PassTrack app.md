<div><p>As the use of pass tracks has been made mandatory for travel abroad, smartphone users can now use the PassTrack app. While, users who do not have a smartphone can also avail the web portal set up by the National Information Technology Board (NITB).</p>

<p>According to NITB, &#8220;Pass Track app by the National Information Technology Board is part of management for traveler coming over to Pakistan.&#8221;</p>

<p>The app aids in recording and tracking of passengers' basic information. The application comes with a scanning option for the National Identity Card (NIC) and passport.</p>

<p>Pakistan Civil Aviation Authority (PCAA) last month issued SOPs for international passengers. As per these SOPs, passengers shall be required to install PassTrack app, downloadable from Google's play store and the Apple store.</p>

<p>Children under age 12, disabled passengers, and high level International delegations referred by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs will be exempted from the app/web data requirement.</p>
