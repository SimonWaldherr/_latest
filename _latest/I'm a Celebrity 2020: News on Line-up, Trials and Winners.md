<div><p>I'm a Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here! informally known as I'm A Celebrity or I'm A Celeb is a British reality TV show that traditionally puts a line-up of celebrities in an Australian jungle camp.</p><p>The celebrities must live in jungle conditions and complete trials in a bid to win meals and votes from the viewers to be crowned king or queen of the jungle at the end of the series. </p><p>In 2020 the winner will be king or queen of the castle, with the series being moved to <a href="https://www.mirror.co.uk/tv/tv-news/breaking-im-celeb-confirm-uk-22578916">Gwrych Castle in Wales</a> as a result of the coronavirus pandemic.</p></div>
