  Camilla Parker Bowles urges people to stay active during the pandemic 'for the sake of our bones' 
description: > 
  The Duchess of Cornwall has told of the importance of staying active for 'the sake of our bones' - even in the midst of a pandemic.Camilla Parker Bowles, 73, made the comments in a video, believed to have been filmed at Clarence House, released to mark World Osteoporosis Day on Tuesday.In the speech, the Duchess tells of her pain at watching her mother succumb to the 'devastating disease'.Earlier today the Duchess spoke to the next recipient of the Duchess of Cornwall Award, Christine Sharp, who is a long standing volunteer of the charity.The Duchess of Cornwall Award exists to recognise an individual for their outstanding contribution in the field of osteoporosis.
