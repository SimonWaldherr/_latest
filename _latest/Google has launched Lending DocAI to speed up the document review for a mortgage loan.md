</p><p>Google is partnering with mortgage software provider Roostify to make Lending DocAI available to customers like Santander Bank and Hamilton Home Loans. The release comes after Google <a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21843982.4/aHR0cHM6Ly92ZW50dXJlYmVhdC5jb20vMjAyMC8wNS8wMS9nb29nbGUtcmVsZWFzZXMtYWktc29sdXRpb24tdG8taGVscC1sZW5kZXJzLXByb2Nlc3MtcGF5Y2hlY2stcHJvdGVjdGlvbi1wcm9ncmFtLWxvYW5zLw/5d233c18f730436f2414784fBf3c53b38" target="_blank" rel="noopener">launched </a>PPP Lending AI in May, which helped lenders enhance application processing for the Small Business Administration's Paycheck Protection Program.</p><p>The new solution brings benefits to mortgage companies' internal operations, while also boosting customer satisfaction. Conventionally, lenders would have to <a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21843982.4/aHR0cHM6Ly92ZW50dXJlYmVhdC5jb20vMjAyMC8xMC8xOS9nb29nbGUtY2xvdWQtbGF1bmNoZXMtbGVuZGluZy1kb2NhaS10by1zdHJlYW1saW5lLW1vcnRnYWdlLWFwcGxpY2F0aW9ucy13aXRoLWFpLw/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB274f3b05" target="_blank" rel="noopener">process</a>&#160;hundreds of pages of paperwork to make a mortgage decision, making this a tedious and lengthy process that's prone to human error.</p><p>The latest solution is a specialized version of Google's existing technology, Document AI, that leverages optical character recognition to extract important information from complex documents. This aims to reduce the mortgage processing time and costs, and can help shorten the decision process to just a few minutes, compared with the weeks it typically takes. Additionally, it streamlines data capture as well as supports regulatory and compliance requirements.</p><p>The mortgage industry is going through a digital transformation process that has been accelerated by the pandemic.&#160;There has been significant innovation within the mortgage industry, ranging from&#160;<a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21843982.4/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubW9ydGdhZ2ViYW5rZXJtYWcuY29tL2ZlYXR1cmVkL2ZpbnRlY2gtcGFydG5lcnNoaXBzLWZhY2lsaXRhdGUtZGlnaXRhbC1hZG9wdGlvbi1kdXJpbmctYW5kLXBvc3QtY292aWQtMTkv/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB481751bb" target="_blank" rel="noopener">online</a>&#160;mortgage applications to&#160;<a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21843982.4/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cubmVyZHdhbGxldC5jb20vYXJ0aWNsZS9tb3J0Z2FnZXMvZWNsb3Npbmctd2hhdC1idXllcnMtc2hvdWxkLWtub3c/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB60c29f00" target="_blank" rel="noopener">signing</a>&#160;mortgage documents electronically. Even before the crisis, the mortgage industry was undergoing changes: HSBC&#160;<a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21843982.4/aHR0cHM6Ly9pbnRlbGxpZ2VuY2UuYnVzaW5lc3NpbnNpZGVyLmNvbS9wb3N0L2hzYmMtdGFwcy1yb29zdGlmeS1mb3ItZGlnaXRhbC1tb3J0Z2FnZS1uYXR3ZXN0LWxhdW5jaGVzLXNlbGZpZS1vbmJvYXJkaW5nLXN0YXJsaW5nLXdlbGNvbWVzLWluY3VtYmVudC10by1tYXJrZXRwbGFjZS0yMDE5LTY_Y2lkPXNlYXJjaA/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB58363b14" target="_blank" rel="noopener">partnered</a> with Roostify for a new digital mortgage platform, while U.S. Bank <a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21843982.4/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuaG91c2luZ3dpcmUuY29tL2FydGljbGVzLzQ5MDY0LXVzLWJhbmstdW52ZWlscy1kaWdpdGFsLW1vcnRnYWdlLWV4cGVyaWVuY2Uv/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB079b39a2" target="_blank" rel="noopener">picked </a>Blend as a partner for a similar venture.</p><p>This move from manual paper-based processes to automated digital ones has been accelerated by the coronavirus pandemic, which has made in-person meetings difficult, if not impossible. It seems that outsourcing such efforts is a route many incumbents are taking, likely because this gives them access to digital experts as well as up-to-date innovations, and streamlines the go-to-market process by using predeveloped solutions.</p><p>Google has high customer awareness and its wide network in its favor to boost adoption of the solution, while the Roostify partnership gives it access to a number of <a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/alternative-nonbank-mortgage-lending">mortgage lenders</a>.</p><p><strong>Want to read more stories like this one?
