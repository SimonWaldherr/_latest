Jamie was a loving son, husband and father," a statement provided to CNN from Robert Redford's publicist Cindi Berger read. "His legacy lives on through his children, art, filmmaking and devoted passion to conservation and the environment. Robert Redford is mourning with his family during this difficult time and asks for privacy." </p><a></a><p>James Redford's wife, Kyle, <a href="https://twitter.com/kyleredford/status/1317232446849437697?s=20" target="_blank"> tweeted the news on Friday</a> along with photos of him and their family.</p><p id="ad_rect_atf_02" class="ad-ad_rect_atf_02 ad-refresh-default"></p><p></p><p></p><ul class="cn cn-list-hierarchical-xs cn--idx-5 cn-zoneAdContainer"></ul><p class="zn-body__paragraph">"Jamie died today. We're heartbroken," she wrote. "I don't know what we would've done w/o them over the past 2yrs."</p><a></a><p>Kyle Redford <a href="https://www.sltrib.com/artsliving/2020/10/19/james-redford-filmmaker/" target="_blank">told The Salt Lake Tribune</a> her husband died of bile-duct cancer in his liver.</p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">David James Redford, who was known as "Jamie," was the third of four children born to Robert Redford and his former wife, historian Lola Van Wagenen. </p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">James Redford graduated with a bachelor's degree in creative writing and film from the University of Colorado-Boulder and a master's degree in literature from Northwestern University. </p><a></a><p>He and his father co-founded The Redford Center in 2005 which <a href="https://redfordcenter.org/about-us" target="_blank"> according to its site </a> "uses impact-driven film and media to accelerate environmental and climate justice, solutions and repair."</p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">The younger Redford served as the organization's chairman where he called on his skills as a writer, director and producer for film and television.</p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">"I come from a long line of storytellers, so the idea of being compelled to make sense of things is essential to who I am and how I see the world," he said.</p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">He wrote, directed and produced more than a dozen films, including the award-winning documentaries "Watershed," "Toxic Hot Seat" and "Mann v. Ford."</p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">After undergoing two liver transplants, he launched the James Redford Institute for Transplant Awareness, a nonprofit seeking to educate the public about the need for organ and tissue donation. </p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">Redford reveled in his outreach.</p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">"I just love meeting people and hearing their stories," he said. "Being in the documentary filmmaking world ensures your life is one long continuing education course. You're constantly learning more &#8212; not only about topics but also about human nature.
