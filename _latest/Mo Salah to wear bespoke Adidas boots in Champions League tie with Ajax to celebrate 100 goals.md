title: > 
  Mo Salah to wear bespoke Adidas boots in Champions League tie with Ajax to celebrate 100 goals 
description: > 
  Liverpool star Mohamed Salah will wear a special pair of Adidas boots in the Champions League this week after hitting 100 goals for the club in the Merseyside Derby against Everton on Saturday.To celebrate Salah's achievement, Adidas have created a bespoke version of the X Ghosted football boots for him to wear in Liverpool's Champions League fixture against Ajax on Wednesday.Salah has, however, tried and tested his boots in training on Monday, but will be looking to break them in properly with a goal on Wednesday in their Champions League group stage opener.Salah will wear the boots in Liverpool's Champions League fixture against Ajax on WednesdayCalled 'Mo's Golden Touch' the boots are gold and white with 'Salah100' down the sideThe Reds will head over to the Eredivisie side after a dramatic Premier League clash with Everton that ended 2-2 on Saturday.Liverpool will be without Virgil van Dyke for their Champions League tie and could be forced to play without Thiago Alcantara - who is currently being assessed after after sustaining a knock against The Toffees.
