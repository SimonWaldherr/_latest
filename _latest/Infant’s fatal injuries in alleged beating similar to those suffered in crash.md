<div><p>A Milwaukee man has been accused of beating his 4-month-old son so badly that the fatal injuries are similar to those suffered in a high-speed crash, according to a report.</p>
 
<p>Ky&#8217;mir Williams, who suffered severe hemorrhaging, was taken off life support on Friday, <a href="https://www.fox6now.com/news/4-month-old-died-from-severe-abuse-milwaukee-father-in-custody" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Fox6 News reported</a>.</p>
<p>&#8220;He was a bundle of joy to our family,&#8221; a relative told the news outlet.</p>
<p>The boy&#8217;s father, 28-year-old Ramel Echols, noticed that his son was &#8220;throwing up blood and milk,&#8221; and called the mother.</p>
<p>When she told him to call 911, police said he waited and eventually took Ky&#8217;mir to the hospital.</p>
<p>Doctors said his injuries were &#8220;consistent with an injury seen in a high-speed motor vehicle accident&#8221; with &#8220;bleeding on the brain&#8221; and &#8220;bruising to the abdomen.&#8221;</p>
<p>Echols, who provided no explanation for the injuries, has been charged with child abuse and neglect, with homicide charges expected.</p>
