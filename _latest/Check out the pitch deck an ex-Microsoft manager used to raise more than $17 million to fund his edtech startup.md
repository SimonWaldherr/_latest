<p>Last week Knowledgehook, the edtech platform cofounded by former Microsoft manager Travis Ratnam, raised almost $18 million in a funding round backed by Alexandria Corp and Nelson Education.&#160;</p><p>Edtech has seen a surge in demand around the world amid the COVID-19 pandemic, with VCs <a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/uk-startup-investment-bounced-back-june-health-and-edtech-2020-8">pouring money</a> into remote learning startups which have allowed individuals and institutions alike to stick to their lesson plans.&#160;</p><p>Knowledgehook incorporates AI technology to help teachers and parents alike track pupils' progression in math.
