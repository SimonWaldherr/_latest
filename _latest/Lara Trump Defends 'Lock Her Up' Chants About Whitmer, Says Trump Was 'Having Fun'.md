<div><p>Lara Trump on Sunday defended calls for Michigan Gov. <a href="https://www.huffpost.com/topic/gretchen-whitmer" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Gretchen Whitmer</a> to be <a href="https://www.huffpost.com/entry/gretchen-whitmer-lock-her-up-michigan-trump_n_5f8b70ffc5b66ee9a5f099eb" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">locked up that came during a rally</a> for her father-in-law, President <a href="https://www.huffpost.com/news/topic/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Donald Trump</a>&#160;on Saturday. She claimed the calls had &#8220;nothing to do with&#8221; a recent kidnapping plot against Whitmer and that Trump was merely having fun.</p><p>&#8220;He wasn&#8217;t doing anything, I don&#8217;t think, to provoke people to threaten this woman, at all,&#8221; Lara Trump, who serves as a senior Trump campaign adviser, said of the president on <a href="https://twitter.com/CNNSotu/status/1317820397627281408" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">CNN&#8217;s &#8220;State of the Union.&#8221;</a> &#8220;He was having fun at a Trump rally. And quite frankly, there are bigger issues than this.&#8221;&#160;</p><p>Her dismissal of the chants came hours after a crowd of Trump supporters in Michigan called for the governor to be locked up in response to the state&#8217;s handling of the ongoing coronavirus pandemic.</p><blockquote class="twitter-tweet" align="center"><p lang="en" dir="ltr">When asked if the President should tone down his rhetoric against Michigan Gov. Gretchen Whitmer, Trump campaign senior adviser Lara Trump says, "He wasn't doing anything, I don't think, to provoke people to threaten this woman at all. He was having fun at a Trump rally" <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/CNNSOTU?src=hash&amp;ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">#CNNSOTU</a> <a href="https://t.co/Hm9GIWeH5d">pic.twitter.com/Hm9GIWeH5d</a></p>&#8212; State of the Union (@CNNSotu) <a href="https://twitter.com/CNNSotu/status/1317820397627281408?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">October 18, 2020</a></blockquote>
 
<p>&#8220;You&#8217;ve got to get your governor to open up your state, OK? And get your schools open. Get your schools open. The schools have to be open, right?&#8221; Trump told the crowd as some in the group began chanting, &#8220;Lock her up!&#8221;</p><p>Trump appeared to laugh at the chant. He added: &#8220;Lock &#8217;em all up.&#8221;</p><p>Whitmer, who authorities said was the victim of a foiled armed abduction by Trump-supporting militia members earlier this month, <a href="https://twitter.com/GovWhitmer/status/1317589850527322112" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">emphasized the seriousness of the president&#8217;s behavior</a>, declaring on Twitter shortly after the incident: &#8220;It needs to stop.&#8221;</p><blockquote class="twitter-tweet" align="center"><p lang="en" dir="ltr">This is exactly the rhetoric that has put me, my family, and other government officials&#8217; lives in danger while we try to save the lives of our fellow Americans. It needs to stop. <a href="https://t.co/EWkNQx3Ppx">https://t.co/EWkNQx3Ppx</a></p>&#8212; Governor Gretchen Whitmer (@GovWhitmer) <a href="https://twitter.com/GovWhitmer/status/1317589850527322112?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">October 17, 2020</a></blockquote>
 
<p>&#8220;This is exactly the rhetoric that has put me, my family and other government officials&#8217; lives in danger while we try to save the lives of our fellow Americans,&#8221; she said, while sharing a video of Trump&#8217;s behavior at the rally.</p><p>Asked whether the rhetoric should be toned down, Laura Trump argued that the comments about Whitmer had &#8220;nothing to do with this attack against her.&#8221; She further argued that she and her family receive their own threats, suggesting that makes it OK.</p><p>Six members of a Michigan militia were arrested earlier this month and charged with plotting to kidnap Whitmer in response to significant restrictions she placed on the state to try to stop the coronavirus from spreading.&#160;Separately, seven others were charged for allegedly seeking to storm the Michigan Capitol and instigate a &#8220;civil war,&#8221;&#160;according to an <a href="https://apnews.com/article/michigan-checks-and-balances-archive-gretchen-whitmer-da09ca66cd8d5f36722021d3593425ff" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">affidavit released by the FBI</a>.</p><p>After Whitmer condemned Trump for encouraging violence among his supporters with hate speech and support of violent groups, Trump accused her in an interview on Thursday of <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/donald-trump/whitmer-blasts-trump-s-appalling-response-her-after-feds-foil-n1243535" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">wanting &#8220;to be a dictator.&#8221;</a></p><p>We want to know what you&#8217;re hearing on the ground from the candidates.
