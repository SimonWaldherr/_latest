showdown this Thursday.</p><p>Trump campaign manager Bill Stepien said in the letter that the campaigns had already agreed foreign policy would be the focus of the third debate.</p><p>The topics were announced by moderator and NBC News correspondent Kristen Welker last week: American families, race in America, climate change, national security and leadership.</p><span class="css-u5hxdy-Placeholder
