<div><p class="endmarkEnabled">Tropical storm Epsilon strengthened to a hurricane Tuesday night as it moved on a projected path that could take it close to Bermuda, forecasters said.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Hurricane Epsilon, named from the Greek alphabet because there have been so many storms that <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/news/weather/more-storms-form-atlantic-forecasters-turn-greek-alphabet-n1240511" target="_blank">the traditional names ran out</a>, became the <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/news/weather/tropical-storm-epsilon-forms-atlantic-becoming-26th-named-storm-hurricane-n1243883" target="_blank">earliest 26th named storm </a>on record Monday morning.</p><p></p><p id="taboolaReadMoreBelow"></p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Tuesday night, the stom had maximum sustained winds of 75 mph, making it a Category 1 on the Saffir-Simpson Hurricane Wind Scale, the hurricane center <a href="https://www.nhc.noaa.gov/text/refresh/MIATCPAT2+shtml/210235.shtml?" target="_blank">said in an advisory</a> at 11 p.m.</p><blockquote class="twitter-tweet"><p lang="en" dir="ltr">11 PM AST Tuesday, October 20 Key Messages for Hurricane <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/Epsilon?src=hash&amp;ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">#Epsilon</a>. Tropical storm conditions are possible on Bermuda beginning Thursday. <a href="https://t.co/22MmuRtUyj">https://t.co/22MmuRtUyj</a> <a href="https://t.co/owzBtcSrjr">pic.twitter.com/owzBtcSrjr</a></p>&#8212; National Hurricane Center (@NHC_Atlantic) <a href="https://twitter.com/NHC_Atlantic/status/1318747249111752707?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">October 21, 2020</a></blockquote> <p class="endmarkEnabled">Epsilon, which could strengthen over the next two days, is forecast to be a hurricane when it makes its closest approach to Bermuda late Thursday, the hurricane center said.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Exact details of the track and its intensity near the island were not yet known.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">A tropical storm watch was in effect for Bermuda, and tropical storm conditions could be seen on the island by Thursday, the hurricane center said.</p><p></p><p class="endmarkEnabled">Large swells generated by the storm could affect Bermuda for several days, the hurricane center said in Tuesday night's advisory, which are expected to generate potentially life-threatening surf and rip currents.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">In September, the list of traditional names for hurricanes &#8212; which come from an international committee of the World Meteorological Organization, a United Nations agency &#8212; ran out when Tropical Storm Wilfred formed in the Atlantic.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">That meant that the names then went to the Greek alphabet.</p><p class="endmarkEnabled">The official Atlantic hurricane season doesn't end until Nov. 30, but tropical cyclones can form even later than that. Tropical storm Zeta in 2005 formed in late December.</p></div>
