<div><p>With tier three restrictions set to be introduced for South Yorkshire <a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-54627017" class="css-yidnqd-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">from Saturday,</a> people in Barnsley spoke about what they think of the new rules.</p><p>People had mixed reactions with some saying they were "worried" and others saying stricter measures were "good" because people were breaking the rules.</p><p>The new restrictions will apply to all four local authority areas in South Yorkshire - Barnsley, Doncaster, Rotherham and Sheffield.</p><p>Labour Mayor Dan Jarvis said the move to tier three followed "extensive discussions" with ministers.</p></div>
