If you're looking to provide your guests with an authentic Caribbean experience, consider Anguilla. Thanks to an embargo on cruise ships, casinos and high-rise hotels, the island isn't saturated with quite as many tourist traps as neighboring destinations. Plus, the live music scene will have you and your loved ones celebrating your nuptials into the wee hours of the morning.
