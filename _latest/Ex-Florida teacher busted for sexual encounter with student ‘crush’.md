<div><p>A former Florida teacher admitted to having a &#8220;crush&#8221; on a student after there was a sexual encounter between them at his home, authorities said.</p>
<p>Kristen O&#8217;Brien, who quit her job last month at United Brethren Christ Academy in Holy Hill, was arrested Friday for having inappropriate sexual contact with a student, <a href="https://www.wftv.com/news/local/volusia-county/former-holly-hill-teacher-arrested-after-having-inappropriate-emotional-relationship-with-student-report-says/TPOF6RJZOFHWRHMTKIM3WOD5WY/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">news station WFTV reported</a>.</p>
<p>The 31-year-old was confronted by the boy&#8217;s mother after she noticed concerning messages on his phone, police said.</p>
<p>O&#8217;Brien told the mom that she had an &#8220;inappropriate emotional relationship&#8221; with the student in which she had a &#8220;crush&#8221; on him, authorities said.</p>
<p>She claimed that she had given the boy three options: They could pretend she never told him she had a crush; they could end communication with each other; or they could do whatever they wanted and not care about any repercussions,<a href="https://www.news-journalonline.com/story/news/crime/2020/10/19/teacher-accused-sexual-battery-former-student/5984120002/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"> the Daytona Beach News-Journal reported</a>.</p>
<p>O&#8217;Brien said on one occasion, the boy touched her in a sexual manner while they were under a blanket on the couch at his home, the newspaper reported.</p>
<p>The teacher also admitted at one point she had tried to &#8220;make [the boy] jealous&#8221; by tutoring his brother and taking him to McDonald&#8217;s, police said.</p>
<p>O&#8217;Brien faces charges of lewd and lascivious battery and committing a sexual act on a child older than 12 but younger than 16, the outlets reported. She was released from jail on $50,000 bail.</p>
