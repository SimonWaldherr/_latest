<div><p>A brute shoved a 73-year-old grandma onto the subway tracks during a fight on a Brooklyn platform, police said Monday.</p>
<p>The encounter unfolded around 1:45 p.m. at the C train&#8217;s Clinton-Washington stop, where an unknown man got into a fight with the elderly woman&#8217;s husband and grandson in his 30s, according to police and sources.</p>
<p>The attacker punched and kicked the two men after the younger man asked the assailant to stop smoking on the platform &#8212; and eventually, shoved the 73-year-old onto the tracks, police and sources said.</p>
<p>Thankfully, she was not hit by the train and was expected to recover after being taken by EMS to New York-Presbyterian Brooklyn Methodist Hospital, according to police.</p>
<p>Cops are still hunting for the attacker.</p>
