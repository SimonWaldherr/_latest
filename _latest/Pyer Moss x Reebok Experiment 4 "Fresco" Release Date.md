Pyer Moss and Reebok continue to deliver. The latest Pyer Moss x Reebok Experiment 4 “Fresco” combines Reebok’s historic footwear legacy along with Pyer Moss’ storytelling that is second to none.

The apparel and footwear are embodied by the message of Sankofa and the importance of reclaiming what is yours. A black outsole and heel are accompanied by a white base that is accentuated with blue and pastel green. Hints of yellow are found near the heel and within the lining of the shoe itself.

Take a look at the pair ahead of its release. Be sure to take a look at the pair below. The latest colorway of the Pyer Moss x Reebok Experiment 4 will be available at PyerMoss.com, Reebok.com, and select retailers.
