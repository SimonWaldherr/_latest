<div><p>ABU DHABI &#8211; <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/khabib-nurmagomedov">Khabib Nurmagomedov</a> and <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/justin-gaethje">Justin Gaethje</a> are one step closer to their heavily anticipated UFC 254 clash.</p>
<p>UFC lightweight champ Nurmagomedov (28-0 MMA, 12-0 UFC) and interim titleholder Gaethje (22-2 MMA, 5-2 UFC) squared off Wednesday on Yas Beach following a news conference in support of Saturday&#8217;s pay-per-view event, which takes place at the nearby Flash Forum on Yas Island.</p>
<p>The faceoff is the last time the two athletes are scheduled to appear together prior to Friday&#8217;s ceremonial faceoffs following the morning&#8217;s official weigh-ins.</p>
<p>Check out video of their staredown above.</p>
<p>Saturday&#8217;s UFC 254 streams live on pay-per-view at a special start time of 2 p.m. ET following prelims on ESPN2/ESPN+ and early prelims on UFC Fight Pass/ESPN+.</p>
