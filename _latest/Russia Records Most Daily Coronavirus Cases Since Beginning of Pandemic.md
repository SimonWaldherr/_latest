Russia recorded more than 12,100 new cases of the coronavirus on Friday, marking the highest one-day count since the onset of the pandemic.

The country's coronavirus headquarters reported 12,126 new cases Friday, bringing its total to more than 1.27 million cases. Officials also reported 201 deaths Friday, bringing Russia's death toll to more than 22,250.

The new cases were identified in 85 regions, and 3,272 cases, or 27% of the total new cases, are asymptomatic.

Most of the new cases were reported in Moscow and the surrounding area, with more than 4,000 new cases recorded. Officials report more than 74,000 active cases in the region. St. Petersburg reported the second most number of daily cases on Friday, at 469, bringing its total number of active cases to more than 12,530.
