<div><img class="size-large wp-image-439996 lazy-load" src="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/91/2019/09/khabib-nurmagomedov-dustin-poirier-ufc-242-18.jpg?w=100&amp;h=66" alt width="1000" height="666"><img class="size-large wp-image-439996" src="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/91/2019/09/khabib-nurmagomedov-dustin-poirier-ufc-242-18.jpg?w=1000" alt="" width="1000" height="666"><p>Nurmagomedov is one of three Russian-born champions in UFC history. Bantamweight champ Petr Yan and former heavyweight champ Andrei Arlovski also accomplished the feat.</p>
<p>Nurmagomedov is one of two lightweight champions in UFC history to earn back-to-back title defense victories by submission. Anthony Pettis also accomplished the feat.</p>
<p>Nurmagomedov&#8217;s 28-fight winning streak is the longest among active UFC fighters.</p>
<p>Nurmagomedov&#8217;s 11-fight UFC winning streak at lightweight is the longest active streak in the division.</p>
<p>Nurmagomedov&#8217;s 12-fight UFC winning streak is the longest active streak in the company.</p>
<p>Nurmagomedov is one of two fighters in UFC history to earn 12 consecutive octagon victories to start a career with the organization. Anderson Silva also accomplished the feat.</p>
<p>Nurmagomedov&#8217;s 21 takedowns landed against Abel Trujillo at UFC 160 are a single-fight UFC record.</p>
<p>Nurmagomedov has landed 57 takedowns in UFC lightweight competition, the second most in divisional history behind Gleison Tibau (84).</p>
<p>Nurmagomedov&#8217;s 88 significant ground strikes landed against Michael Johnson at UFC 205 are tied for fourth most in a single UFC fight.</p>
<p>Nurmagomedov absorbs just 1.70 significant strikes per minute in UFC lightweight competition, the second best rate among active fighters in the weight class behind Islam Makhachev (0.76).</p>
<img class="size-large wp-image-517262 lazy-load" src="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/91/2020/05/justin-gaethje-tony-ferguson-ufc-249-5.jpg?w=100&amp;h=73" alt width="1000" height="737"><img class="size-large wp-image-517262" src="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/91/2020/05/justin-gaethje-tony-ferguson-ufc-249-5.jpg?w=1000" alt="" width="1000" height="737"><p>Gaethje is the only fighter MMA history to win titles in UFC and WSOF.</p>
<p>Gaethje has earned 20 of his 22 career victories by stoppage. That includes all five of his UFC wins.</p>
<p>Gaethje is the only fighter in UFC history to land 100 or more significant strikes in each of his first three promotional appearances.</p>
<p>Gaethje lands 7.74 significant strikes per minute in UFC competition, the highest rate in company history.</p>
<p>Gaethje lands 2.36 leg kicks per minute in UFC competition, the highest rate in company history.</p>
<p>Gaethje lands 59.2 percent of significant strike attempts in UFC lightweight competition, the best rate in divisional history.</p>
<p>Gaethje has spent just 17 seconds in bottom position in UFC lightweight contests, the third least among of time in divisional history behind Gregor Gillespie (eight seconds) and Adriano Martins (nine seconds).</p>
<p>Gaethje is the only fighter in UFC history to win at least one fight-night bonus in each of his first seven octagon appearances.</p>
<p>Gaethje has been awarded nine fight-night bonuses in seven UFC appearances.</p>
<p>Gaethje&#8217;s nine UFC fight-night bonuses since 2017 are most of any fighter in the company.</p>
