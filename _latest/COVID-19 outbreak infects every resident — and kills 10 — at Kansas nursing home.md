<div><p>An outbreak of coronavirus at a privately-owned nursing home in Kansas has caused all 62 residents of the facility to become infected with the deadly virus.</p>
<p>A total of 10 residents of the Andbe Home northwest Kansas have perished from the virus, while one person is being treated at a local hospital and 51 others remain in care at the home, according to <a href="https://www.facebook.com/NortonCountyHealthDeptandHomeHealth/photos/a.1207179892627142/3751157051562734/">Norton County Health Department</a>.</p>
<p>A number of staff members have also tested positive for the virus.</p>
<p>The health department said that the remaining stricken residents were being quarantined in their rooms to prevent further spread of the virus.</p>
<p>A total of 72,968 people have contracted coronavirus in Kansas &#8212; 872 deaths have been attributed to the illness, according to the <a href="https://www.coronavirus.kdheks.gov/160/COVID-19-in-Kansas">state department of health</a>.</p>
