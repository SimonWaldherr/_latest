<div><p>Is there anything better than your favorite comfort food?</p>

<p>Yes, there is. Being rewarded every time you enjoy your comfort food!</p>

<p>Thanks to the cool new <strong>McDonald's Rewards Program</strong>, mealtimes can now be double the fun, helping you earn rewards every time you spend at any of their restaurants in the UAE.</p>

<p>The <strong>McDonald's Rewards Program</strong> will reward you with food, beverages and other memorable surprises only on the McDonald&#8217;s App.</p>

<p>What's even more incredible, this is easily one of the most straightforward reward programs out there: No forms, no emails, and no talking to any customer service agent.</p>

<img alt="" class="image photo" src="https://sm.mashable.com/mashable_me/photo/default/22017-sydney-green-square-2020jan07-03106_zz7s.jpg">
More of McDonald's!


<p>All you need to do is download the McDonald's App, from either the App Store or the Google Play Store, register yourself, and you will get auto-enrolled into the Rewards Program and be able to start collecting points right away.</p>

<p>To collect points, scan the QR Code that can be found on the Rewards page of the McDonald&#8217;s App at the restaurant front counter, self-ordering kiosk or at the Drive-Thru while purchasing any meal.</p>

<img alt="" class="image photo" src="https://sm.mashable.com/mashable_me/photo/default/20124-singapore-changiairport-2019oct22-d7a5921_8qz2.jpg">
Know a more awesome app?


<p>You will collect 10 McDonald's Rewards points for every AED 1 you spend, and McDonald&#8217;s will even give you 100 bonus points the first time you scan your Rewards' QR Code.</p>

<p>Depending on how many points you have collected, which you can track on the McDonald&#8217;s App, you will be able to redeem them for more of what you love!</p>
