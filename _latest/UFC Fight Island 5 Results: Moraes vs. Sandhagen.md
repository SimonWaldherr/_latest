MMA Fighting has UFC Fight Island 5 results fo the Moraes vs. Sandhagen event Saturday night, live blog of the main event, and live UFC Fight Island 5 Twitter updates.

In the main event, Marlon Moraes and Cory Sandhagen will square off in a key bantamweight contest. Moraes and Sandhagen have both won four of their past five fights.

Featherweights Edson Barboza and Makwan Amirkhani will clash in the co-main event.

Follow UFC Fight Island 5 results below.

Main Card (ESPN+, 8 p.m. ET)

Marlon Moraes vs. Cory Sandhagen

Ben Rothwell vs. Marcin Tybura

Markus Perez vs. Dricus Du Plessis

Tom Aspinall vs. Alan Baudot
