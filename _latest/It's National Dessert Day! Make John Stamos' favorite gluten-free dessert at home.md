<div><p class="teal-video-wrap"></p><p class="gnt_em_vt"></p><p class="gnt_em_vb gnt_em_vb__te"></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">It's National Dessert Day&#160;&#8211; are you baking yet?&#160;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">In Hollywood, celebs like John Stamos have partnered with&#160;<a href="https://www.americassweetest.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">America&#8217;s Sweetest</a>, a one-day celebrity bake sale&#160;in support of <a href="https://www.ebmrf.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Epidermolysis Bullosa Medical Research Foundation</a>. The "Fuller House" actor joins stars like Courteney Cox, Eva Longoria and Elizabeth Olsen&#160;in collaborating on&#160;their favorite treats from LA&#160;hotspots, from <a href="https://www.portosbakery.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Porto's Bakery</a> to <a href="https://gjusta.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Gjusta</a> (<a href="https://www.americassweetest.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">available Wednesday </a>for $125&#160;in Los Angeles in a delectable sampler box).</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Uncle Jesse's chosen sweet happens to be a gluten-free brownie from <a href="https://valerieconfections.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener" class="gnt_ar_b_a">Valerie Confections</a> topped with his mother-in-law's&#160;'Crispy Caramel Corn Nuts.'&#160;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Want to make it yourself at home? Here's the recipe:</p><h2 class="gnt_ar_b_h2">Valerie Confections' gluten-free 'Layers of Love' brownie&#160;cakes&#160;</h2><img class="gnt_em_img_i" alt="The 'Layers Of Love' treat by John &amp; Caitlin Stamos and Valerie Gordon of Valerie Confections."><p class="gnt_em_img_ccw gnt_em_img_ccw__cap gnt_em_img_ccw__crd"></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p"><strong>Ingredients:</strong></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">1 jar store-bought caramel sauce</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">10 ounces melted chocolate (for finishing)</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Sea salt&#160;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">&#190; cup + 1 tablespoon buckwheat flour</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">&#189; teaspoon kosher salt</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">&#188; teaspoon baking soda</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">4 ounces&#160;salted butter (1 stick of butter)</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">7 ounces unsweetened chocolate</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">2 tablespoons canola oil</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">3 large eggs</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">1 cup (7 ounces) golden brown sugar</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">1 cup (7 ounces) white sugar</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">2 teaspoons vanilla paste</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">'Crispy Caramel Corn Nuts' topping (recipe below)</p><img class="gnt_em_img_i" alt="Stars are joining together for a celebrity bake sale on National Dessert Day to benefit the  Epidermolysis Bullosa Medical Research Foundation."><p class="gnt_em_img_ccw gnt_em_img_ccw__cap gnt_em_img_ccw__crd"></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p"><strong>Instructions:&#160;</strong></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Line a half sheet baking pan with parchment.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Whisk together the buckwheat flour, salt and baking soda in a small bowl.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">In a medium heat-proof bowl, melt the butter, chocolate and canola oil together over indirect heat, set aside to cool.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Whisk eggs, sugars and vanilla paste into the chocolate mixture. When thoroughly combined, add the dry ingredients.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Bake for 30 minutes or until a cake tester comes out with crumbs but not wet.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Allow to cool completely, then chill for 30 minutes.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Use&#160;a 3-inch, round cookie cutter to&#160;create circular brownies.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Place one brownie on a flat surface.&#160;Using a small scoop or teaspoon, scoop out a circle shape from the brownie, making sure you do not hollow it completely.&#160;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Fill the void with caramel sauce.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Layer a second brownie on top of the caramel-filled bottom layer.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Repeat with remaining brownies.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Pour melted chocolate over the brownie towers, so it drizzles attractively.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Top with a caramel corn snack and finish with a bit of sea salt.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Refrigerate for about 5 minutes, or until the chocolate is set.</p><img class="gnt_em_img_i" alt='John Stamos and wife Caitlin hit the "Once Upon A Time...In Hollywood" premiere together in 2019.'><p class="gnt_em_img_ccw gnt_em_img_ccw__cap gnt_em_img_ccw__crd"></p><h2 class="gnt_ar_b_h2">'Crispy Caramel Corn Nuts' topping</h2><p class="gnt_ar_b_p"><strong>Ingredients:</strong></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">7-8 ounces puffed corn (found in the snack/chip aisle at most grocery stores)</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">1 cup butter</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">1 1/2 cups dark brown sugar</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">1/2 cup&#160;light Karo syrup (or light corn syrup)</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">1 teaspoon&#160;baking soda</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">1 teaspoon&#160;pure vanilla extract</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p"><strong>Instructions:</strong></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Pre-heat oven to 250 degrees. Spray a large roasting tray with cooking spray. Pour puffed corn&#160;into the pan.</p><p id="~ad-slot-7103-usatoday-native-article_link-lifestyle-food-2" class="gnt_x_sl gnt_x_al"></p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add brown sugar and Karo (or corn syrup) and mix well. Bring mixture to a boil, stirring constantly and cook for an additional 2 minutes.</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Remove from heat and stir in baking soda and vanilla extract:&#160;the mixture will foam and expand. Carefully pour mixture over the corn and toss to mix.&#160;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Bake for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes. Pour onto wax paper or foil sprayed with cooking spray. Let cool at least 30 minutes.&#160;</p><p class="gnt_ar_b_p">Break into bite size pieces and store in a closed container until use.&#160;</p><a class="gnt_em gnt_em__lp gnt_em_gl gnt_em__el" href="/picture-gallery/life/2019/02/26/amazing-easy-vegan-recipes-desserts/2779289002/"><img class="gnt_em_gl_i" alt="" width="660" height="372"><p class="gnt_em_gl_i_nt"></p><p class="gnt_em_gl_vg"></p><p class="gnt_em_t gnt_em_t__hb"></p></a></div>
