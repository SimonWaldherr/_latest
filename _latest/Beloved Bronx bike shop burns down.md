<div><a></a><p>Multiple firefighters were injured while battling a four-alarm blaze at a beloved <a href="https://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/ny-nyc-fire-bike-stop-destroyed-20201020-ealdyd3ckbdlnc4gzy4ntsbwx4-story.html%20">Arrow Cycle</a> bike shop in the Bronx Monday night, Oct. 19, 2020. Nearly 200 firefighters were called to the scene of the fire on White Plains Rd. Investigators are still trying to figure out the cause of the fire. Arrow Cycles had been a community staple in Wakefield for generations. In 1975, John Ventarola took the shop over from his father, who opened the store around 1952.</p></div>
