A man was shot dead outside an East Village restaurant early Sunday, leaving a trail of blood down block as he collapsed near a CitiBike stand, police and witnesses said. The 26-year-old victim was hit once in the chest on the corner of E. 13th St. and Avenue A after getting into a fight with another man around 1:20 a.m., cops said. (Theodore Parisienne/for New York Daily News)
