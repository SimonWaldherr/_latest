<p>So far this year, 138 SPACs, aka "blank-check firms," have raised at least $53.8 billion, according to the website SPAC Research. There shell companies now have at least $67 billion to spend on bringing other companies public, the data provider said.</p><p>There's an <a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/top-spac-underwriters-law-firms-investors-blank-check-volume-fees-2020-9?r=spac-teaser" target="_blank" rel="noopener">entire ecosystem of advisers, salespeople, and lawyers</a> increasingly pitching blank-check companies to investment platforms and wealthy people.
