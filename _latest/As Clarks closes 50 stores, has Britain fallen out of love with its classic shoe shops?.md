The retail lockdown prompted by the coronavirus outbreak in the spring saw Clarks stores shutter like all others, but ahead of that, in November 2019 it had already warned that sales were falling.

The glittery world of ‘shoe business’ has transformed dramatically in the last decade, with the strengthening of prominent quality and fashion driven rivals such as Kurt Geiger and Office on the high street, as well as a dramatic fashion shift towards wearing trainers for all occasions. The online shopping revolution has also changed the way we shop for shoes - the gamble which comes of not trying pairs on in person, perhaps doesn’t feel so great when there are free deliveries and returns on offer, with no consequence for ordering multiple sizes.
