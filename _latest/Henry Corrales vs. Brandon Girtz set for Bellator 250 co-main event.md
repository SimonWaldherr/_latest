<div><p id="vCjMHO"><a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/3270/henry-corrales">Henry Corrales</a> welcomes <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/1994/brandon-girtz">Brandon Girtz</a> to the featherweight division next week.</p>
<p id="9E9fU1">MMA Fighting confirmed with an official that Corrales (17-5) and Girtz (16-9) will meet in the co-main event of Bellator 250 on Oct. 29 at Mohegan Sun Arena in Uncasville, Conn. The matchup was first reported by <a href="https://twitter.com/LynchOnSports/status/1313534068038889473">The Scrap</a>.</p>
<p id="Ik1Lqe">In his past two outings, Corrales has dropped decisions to <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/3227/juan-archuleta">Juan Archuleta</a> and <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/1762/darrion-caldwell">Darrion Caldwell</a>. The loss to Caldwell snapped a five-fight winning streak for Corrales that saw him knock off the likes of <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/4100/aaron-pico">Aaron Pico</a>, <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/449/georgi-karakhanyan">Georgi Karakhanyan</a>, and <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/2733/noad-lahat">Noad Lahat</a>.</p>
<p id="UDSfs7">This will be Girtz&#8217;s first fight at featherweight inside the Bellator cage. Girtz has alternated wins and losses in his past five fights, losing to <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/548/myles-jury">Myles Jury</a>, <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/455/michael-chandler">Michael Chandler</a>, and <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/1658/derek-campos">Derek Campos</a>, while defeating <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/731/saad-awad">Saad Awad</a> and <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/4315/luka-jelcic">Luka Jelcic</a>.</p>
<p id="7WJKxp">Bellator 250 is scheduled to be headlined by a battle for a vacant middleweight title between current welterweight champion <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/298/douglas-lima">Douglas Lima</a> and former middleweight champion <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/50/gegard-mousasi">Gegard Mousasi</a>. The main card airs live on CBS Sports Network.</p>
