</p><p>For consumers, buying and selling cryptocurrencies will carry fees based on PayPal's <a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21857531.4/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cucGF5cGFsLmNvbS91cy93ZWJhcHBzL21wcC9wYXlwYWwtZmVlcw/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB360c77b0" target="_blank" rel="noopener">rate structure</a>. PayPal plans to introduce the offering into select international markets and will expand cryptocurrency features into Venmo in H1 2021. The new service&#8212;enabled thanks to PayPal's conditional Bitlicense from the New York State Department of Financial Services&#8212;is being <a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21857531.4/aHR0cHM6Ly90ZWNoY3J1bmNoLmNvbS8yMDIwLzEwLzIxL3BheXBhbC10by1sZXQteW91LWJ1eS1hbmQtc2VsbC1jcnlwdG9jdXJyZW5jaWVzLWluLXRoZS11cy8/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB8e87ffcf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">launched</a>&#160;in partnership with Paxos, which will enable back-end trading and custody.&#160;</p><p>PayPal's move into the space could popularize and accelerate acceptance of crypto payments.&#160;Market volatility, transaction cost and time, and their <a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21857531.4/aHR0cHM6Ly9uZXdzcm9vbS5wYXlwYWwtY29ycC5jb20vMjAyMC0xMC0yMS1QYXlQYWwtTGF1bmNoZXMtTmV3LVNlcnZpY2UtRW5hYmxpbmctVXNlcnMtdG8tQnV5LUhvbGQtYW5kLVNlbGwtQ3J5cHRvY3VycmVuY3k/5d233c18f730436f2414784fC7e85bc74" target="_blank" rel="noopener">limited utility</a>&#160;for transactions have prevented widespread adoption of cryptocurrencies as a payment method and stymied merchant acceptance in the past few years. But with more players like&#160;<a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21857531.4/aHR0cHM6Ly9pbnRlbGxpZ2VuY2UuYnVzaW5lc3NpbnNpZGVyLmNvbS9wb3N0L2FudC1maW5hbmNpYWwtbWlnaHQtYWNxdWlyZS13b3JsZGZpcnN0LWNoaW1waW9uLWNhbi1ib29zdC1jcnlwdG8tbWVyY2hhbnQtYWNjZXB0YW5jZS12ZW5tby11cGRhdGVzLXVzZXItYWdyZWVtZW50LTIwMTgtMTI_Y2lkPXNlYXJjaA/5d233c18f730436f2414784fBce2608b1" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Chimpion</a>,&#160;<a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21857531.4/aHR0cHM6Ly9pbnRlbGxpZ2VuY2UuYnVzaW5lc3NpbnNpZGVyLmNvbS9wb3N0L3dlY2hhdC1wYXktbGVhbnMtb24tY2hpbmVzZS1uZXcteWVhci12ZW5lenVlbGEtYmVjb21pbmctYS1kaWdpdGFsLXBheW1lbnRzLWhvdGJlZC1jb2luYmFzZS1mYWNpbGl0YXRlcy1tZXJjaGFudC1jcnlwdG8tYWNjZXB0YW5jZS0yMDE4LTI_Y2lkPXNlYXJjaA/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB2bf2f832" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Coinbase</a>, and <a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21857531.4/aHR0cHM6Ly9pbnRlbGxpZ2VuY2UuYnVzaW5lc3NpbnNpZGVyLmNvbS9wb3N0L2x5ZnQtbWFzdGVyY2FyZC1vZmZlci1yZXdhcmRzLWRlYml0LWNhcmQtZm9yLWx5ZnQtZHJpdmVycy1hbWV4cy1yZXN5LWFjcXVpc2l0aW9uLXRvLWRlZXBlbi1kaW5pbmctcHVzaC1mbGV4YS1lbmFibGVzLWN1c3RvbWVycy10by1wYXktYnktY3J5cHRvLTIwMTktNT9jaWQ9c2VhcmNo/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB0a45c59f" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Flexa </a>facilitating crypto transactions, acceptance has begun to grow. PayPal boasts 26 million merchants worldwide, so the feature could help dramatically boost the acceptance rate by enabling these transactions at a much wider set of players.&#160;</p><p>PayPal's&#160;<a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21857531.4/aHR0cHM6Ly9pbnRlbGxpZ2VuY2UuYnVzaW5lc3NpbnNpZGVyLmNvbS9wb3N0L3BheXBhbC1hbmQtdW5pb25wYXktcGFydG5lci10by1ib29zdC1hY2NlcHRhbmNlLWxlZ2FjeS1yZW1pdHRhbmNlLXBsYXllcnMtbGF1bmNoLWdsb2JhbC1wYXJ0bmVyc2hpcHMtc2FtZS1kYXktYWNoLXNldC10by1yZWFjaC1uZXctaGVpZ2h0cy0yMDIwLTE_Y2lkPXNlYXJjaA/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB8b95f616" target="_blank" rel="noopener">system</a>&#160;is both wide-spanning and uniquely robust&#8212;we think it could benefit the firm in three ways:&#160;&#160;</p><ul><li><strong>PayPal can position itself to capitalize on central banks' interest in cryptocurrencies.&#160;</strong>Global central banks have <a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21857531.4/aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuY25iYy5jb20vMjAyMC8wMS8yMS9nbG9iYWwtY2VudHJhbC1iYW5rcy1hcmUtcGFydG5lcmluZy11cC10by1leHBsb3JlLWRpZ2l0YWwtY3VycmVuY2llcy5odG1s/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB7e262b4c" target="_blank" rel="noopener">expressed interest </a>in implementing digital fiat currencies, which are similar to cryptos in some ways: Central banks in the UK, Japan, and other markets have already teamed up with the Bank for International Settlements (BIS) to assess use cases for these types of currencies. PayPal's offering may appeal to customers who want to buy and sell cryptocurrencies, helping it mirror Square's success in the space and attract customers interested in crypto who might have otherwise gone to another player. Furthermore, because the offering will enable crypto acceptance for its global merchant network, it might be a useful payment method for traveling customers, helping PayPal grow its user base further.</li><li><strong>The new offering could represent a lucrative revenue opportunity.&#160;</strong>If the buying and selling portion of PayPal's offering takes off, it can cash in big with its fee structure&#8212;a&#160;<a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21857531.4/aHR0cHM6Ly9pbnRlbGxpZ2VuY2UuYnVzaW5lc3NpbnNpZGVyLmNvbS9wb3N0L2Nhc2gtYXBwLWZ1ZWxlZC1zcXVhcmVzLXBlcmZvcm1hbmNlLWluLXEyLXByaW1lLWRheS1pbi1pbmRpYS13aWxsLXByb3ZpZGUtbGVhcm5pbmdzLWZvci1hbWF6b24tZ3JhYi1leHBhbmRzLWZpbmFuY2lhbC1zZXJ2aWNlcy10b29scy1pbi1zZWEtMjAyMC04P2NpZD1zZWFyY2g/5d233c18f730436f2414784fBb3398bc8" target="_blank" rel="noopener">revenue driver</a>&#160;for Square as well&#8212;which is notably more expensive compared with other platforms, like&#160;<a href="https://e.businessinsider.com/click/21857531.4/aHR0cHM6Ly9oZWxwLmNvaW5iYXNlLmNvbS9lbi9jb2luYmFzZS90cmFkaW5nLWFuZC1mdW5kaW5nL3ByaWNpbmctYW5kLWZlZXMvZmVlcy5odG1s/5d233c18f730436f2414784fB6e952214" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Coinbase</a>.
