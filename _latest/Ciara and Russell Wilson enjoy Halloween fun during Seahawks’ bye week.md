<div><p>Ciara and Russell Wilson are getting into the Halloween spirit from the comforts of home.</p>
<p>Taking to Instagram on Saturday, the 34-year-old singer shared a photo of son Future, 6, and daughter Sienna, 3, decorating spooky gingerbread houses in the kitchen, with some assistance from the 31-year-old Seattle Seahawks quarterback.</p>
 
<p>&#8220;Family Halloween Festivities @DangeRussWilson. #HauntedHouses #PumpkinPatch #Quarantine Style:),&#8221; Ciara captioned the family photo.</p>
<p>Earlier in the day, Wilson &#8212; who is on a bye this week &#8212; posted an adorable video of his family picking pumpkins from an at-home patch. In the clip, Ciara is overheard asking her kids which pumpkin they should select for their baby brother, 2-month-old Win Harrison.</p>
 
<p>&#8220;Pumpkin Patch at the crib!&#8221; Wilson posted to social media, adding the hashtag &#8220;Quarantine Life.&#8221;</p>
<p>Married since 2016, Ciara and Wilson are parents to Sienna and Win, <a href="https://pagesix.com/2020/07/24/ciara-and-russell-wilson-welcome-baby-boy-win/">whom they welcomed in July</a>. Ciara shares Future with her ex, recording artist Future.</p>
<p>To kick off the 2020 NFL season, <a href="https://nypost.com/2020/09/13/ciara-posts-tribute-to-husband-russell-wilson-on-nfl-week-1/?_ga=2.182290087.1064371662.1603023695-408226937.1554312161">Ciara penned a tribute</a> to Wilson on Instagram, calling the quarterback the &#8220;hardest working man&#8221; she knows.</p>
<p>Currently in his ninth NFL season, Wilson is already at the center&#160;of <a href="https://nypost.com/2020/10/03/seahawks-russell-wilson-finally-has-shot-to-get-mvp-love/">some MVP conversations</a>. In five games, Russell has thrown for 1,502 yards with 19 touchdowns and three interceptions.</p>
<p>The Seahawks are currently 5-0 and sit atop the NFC West.</p>
