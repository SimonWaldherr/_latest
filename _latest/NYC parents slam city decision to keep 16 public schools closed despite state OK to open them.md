“Families with schools in the yellow zones have been caught in a back and forth between the mayor and the governor and are having their lives dictated not by reasons, facts or logic but by meaningless bureaucracy and power plays,” said State Sen. Andrew Gounardes (D-Brooklyn) at a Thursday rally in front of P.S. 682. “There is no good reason to have 16 public schools in the yellow zone closed while the other 308 schools in the yellow zone remain open with increased testing.”
