                                <p>The government on Monday called in representatives of Facebook, Twitter, Google, TikTok, Snapchat, and other social media firms for a meeting on "cyber-Islamism," asking them to take more responsibility for content on their platforms.&#160;</p><p>Four students in France <a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/france-teacher-beheaded-prophet-muhammad-cartoons-students-detained-2020-10">have been detained</a> in connection with the attack on Paty. The Paris police later shot dead the suspected attacker &#8212; identified by local media as Abdoulakh Anzorov, an 18-year-old Chechen refugee &#8212; near the scene of the attack.&#160;</p><p>Paty, a 47-year-old teacher, had <a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/teacher-beheaded-in-paris-after-showing-cartoons-of-prophet-muhammad-2020-10">shown his pupil's caricatures of the Prophet Muhammad</a> from the satirical newspaper Charlie Hebdo as part of an obligatory "moral and civil education" course before he was decapitated.</p><p>A father of one of the pupils at the school, had posted three videos of Paty before the killing, asking for him to be fired and listing the school's address, <a href="https://www.ft.com/content/b2af266c-5a84-4ddd-8d49-ab41f29a7b06?shareType=nongift">per the Financial Times</a>.</p><p>In the third video, the man was accompanied by Abdelhakim Sefrioui, an Islamist militant already deemed a security risk by French intelligence.&#160;</p><p>Anzorov tweeted after the attack: "From Abdullah, the servant of Allah, to Macron, the leader of the infidels, I executed one of your hell dogs who dared to belittle Muhammad."</p><p>G&#233;rald Darmanin, interior minister, said Monday there were 80 investigations into those who justified Paty's death and suspected extremists accused of spreading online hate, per <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/world/2020/oct/19/samuel-paty-french-police-raid-dozens-of-islamist-groups">The Guardian</a>.<a href="https://www.ft.com/content/b2af266c-5a84-4ddd-8d49-ab41f29a7b06?shareType=nongift"></a></p><p>Paty's killing comes just three weeks after <a href="https://www.businessinsider.com/teacher-beheaded-in-paris-after-showing-cartoons-of-prophet-muhammad-2020-10">two journalists were stabbed</a> outside the former offices of the satirical weekly Charlie Hebdo.</p></div>
