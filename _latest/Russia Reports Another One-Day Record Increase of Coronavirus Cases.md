Russia reported on Tuesday another record for the number of daily coronavirus cases with nearly 14,000 new infections.

The country's coronavirus headquarters reported Tuesday that 13,868 new cases of COVID-19 were reported over the past day throughout 85 regions, with 3,892, or 28.1%, displaying no symptoms.

The country now reports more than 1.32 million cases. Additionally over the past day, 244 people died, bringing the death toll to more than 22,960 people.

Moscow has been the hardest hit city in Russia, with 4,618 new cases reported on Tuesday, bringing the city's total to more than 339,430. More than 69,240 of the cases are currently active. The city also has the highest death toll, with more than 5,680 people dying from COVID-19.

According to the country's virus headquarters, more than 172,000 coronavirus tests were conducted over the last day, bringing the country's total to more than 51.3 million tests.

Despite the surge in new virus cases, Anna Popova, the head of Russia's health and consumer rights agency Rospotrebnadzor, said "there is no sense" in shutting down the economy, and Russia's Ministry of Education does not intend to switch to remote learning.
