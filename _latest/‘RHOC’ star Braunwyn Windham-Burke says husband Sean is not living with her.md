<div><p>Click to email this to a friend (Opens in new window)</p><p>Click to share on Twitter (Opens in new window)</p><p>Click to share on Facebook (Opens in new window)</p><p>Braunwyn Windham-Burke and her husband, Sean Burke, are &#8220;redefining&#8221; their marriage, and part of that means living apart.</p>
<p>The &#8220;Real Housewives of Orange County&#8221; star recently <a href="https://www.etonline.com/media/videos/braunwyn-windham-burke-sets-the-record-straight-on-her-marriage-exclusive-154880" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">confirmed to &#8220;Entertainment Tonight&#8221;</a> while giving a tour of her home that Sean, 52, is &#8220;not living here right now.&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;Love him dearly,&#8221; she added. &#8220;We haven&#8217;t gotten along this well in a long time. We&#8217;re redefining what marriage means to us. We&#8217;re not following the old, antiquated rules anymore.&#8221;</p>
<p>Although they are not living together, it doesn&#8217;t appear that the couple has split. They both recently shared loving photos together on their social media.</p>
<p>Less than a week ago, Sean <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/CGTtD0CBk3h/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">captioned a pic</a> of the pair from Puerto Rico, &#8220;The special moments in life.&#8221;</p>
<p>On Sunday, Braunwyn also <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/CGgCRNnF1bw/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">posted photos</a> of them looking cozy, and captioned the slide show with a red heart.</p>
<p>While the &#8220;RHOC&#8221; star said she and her husband are in an open marriage, she told &#8220;ET&#8221; that there are &#8220;no other men in my life.&#8221;</p>
<p>Braunwyn and Sean have been married for 20 years, and are the parents of seven children.</p>
