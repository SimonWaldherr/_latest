<div><p>People began lining up before dawn in downtown Milwaukee on Tuesday, the first day of early in-person voting in Wisconsin. </p>

<p>Like millions of Americans who decided to vote early, they were prepared to wait. Concerns about mail voting and the coronavirus have sent people to the polls earlier than ever.</p>

<p>In Milwaukee, many voters dragged out their parkas and gloves for the first time this season and settled in to wait.</p>
