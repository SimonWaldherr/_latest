<div><p>Franks for nothing! A Manhattan socialite &#8212; who was once...</p><p>A young girl and two others were seriously hurt when a commercial tree trimming truck hit a school bus in upstate Orange County Wednesday morning, authorities said.</p>
<p>The girl, who was sitting behind the bus driver, is hospitalized in critical but stable condition after the 8:20 a.m. crash in the Town of New Windsor, local police <a href="http://newwindsor-ny.gov/Police/Press-Releases" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">said in a statement</a>. Her name and age were not immediately released.</p>
<p>The bus driver and the truck driver are both hospitalized in critical condition.</p>
<p>The crash happened on Route 207 when the truck, heading west, crossed over the double yellow line and struck the Washingtonville School District bus that was heading east, cops said.</p>
<p>Fire and rescue units from four separate departments responded and extricated the three most serious victims, authorities said.</p>
<p>Seven students walked off the bus and were brought to the hospital to be seen by medical professionals. They have since been treated and released.</p>
<p>The district <a href="https://www.pix11.com/news/local-news/northern-suburbs/injuries-reported-in-new-windsor-school-bus-crash-police" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">confirmed to PIX 11</a> that the bus was from Little Britain Elementary school.</p>
<p>Orange County Executive Steven Neuhaus told the station that the scene was &#8220;horrific&#8221; and described the crash as &#8220;probably the worst I&#8217;ve seen.&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;We&#8217;re hopeful that everyone is going to make a full recovery,&#8221; Neuhaus said.</p>
<p>The accident remains under investigation, authorities said.</p>
