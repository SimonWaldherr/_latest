<div><p>Eagles fans once booed Santa Claus, but they won&#8217;t ruin...</p><p>Police officers at the scene of the shooting in Brooklyn today.</p><p>A drive-by shooting in East Flatbush left two teenage boys and a 20-year-old man injured on Tuesday night, and the NYPD was on the lookout for the shooter.</p>
<p>The shooting took place in front of an apartment building at 397 East 49th Street.</p>
<p>A gunman opened fire from inside a car, hitting a 16-year-old in both legs, grazing a 17-year-old in the face and hitting a 20-year-old man in the stomach.</p>
<p>The victims are being treated at the hospital and all of them are expected to survive their injuries, police say.</p>
<p>It&#8217;s unknown why the victims were targeted, and the shooter remains at large after the vehicle fled in an unknown direction.</p>
