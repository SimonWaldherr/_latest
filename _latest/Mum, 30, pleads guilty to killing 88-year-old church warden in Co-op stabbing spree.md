 <p>     </p> <p>Michael Jones QC, prosecuting, told the court the pleas were acceptable to the Crown as at the time of the incident Radcliffe was suffering from schizophrenia.</p> <p>Mr Jones told the court Radcliffe had been &#8220;profoundly mentally ill and unwell&#8221; at the time of the offence.</p> <p>Radcliffe will be sentenced on Wednesday at Merthyr Crown Court.</p> <p>In the wake of Mr Rees' killing his nephew said the 88-year-old&#160; "would do anything for anyone".</p> <p>Michael Smith, from Pontypridd, said he reacted with "absolute disbelief" when he heard what had happened to his uncle.</p> <p>"It's incredibly upsetting," he said.</p>

I hope you are at peace now.
