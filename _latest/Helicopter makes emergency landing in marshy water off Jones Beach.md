<div><p>A helicopter made an emergency landing in marshy water off Jones Beach Wednesday afternoon, police said.</p>
<p>The whirlybird landed in the low water near the Loop Parkway around 4:15 p.m., a Nassau County Police Department spokesman said.</p>
<p>The pilot &#8212; the only person aboard &#8212; received a minor laceration, the spokesman said. A Merrick Fire Department boat took the him to a nearby U.S. Coast Guard station.</p>
<p>The pilot may have been having navigational problems due to heavy fog, police said.</p>
