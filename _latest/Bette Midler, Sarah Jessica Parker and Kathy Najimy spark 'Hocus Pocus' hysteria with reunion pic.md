<div><p>(CNN) This might be the best thing that's happened to Halloween.</p><p>Bette Midler, Sarah Jessica Parker and Kathy Najimy have reprised their roles as the Sanderson sisters from the hit 1993 movie, "Hocus Pocus."</p><p>Midler on Sunday shared a behind-the-scenes photo of herself alongside Parker and Najimy in character on her Instagram , and social media erupted into a frenzy.</p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">Midler put it best in her caption, "'In Search of the Sanderson Sisters' is the best thing to happen to #Halloween since Hocus Pocus + Reeses Pieces!"</p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">We couldn't agree more.</p><a></a><p>The film has become a cult classic, and fans have been begging for a sequel for years. There's <a href="https://www.cnn.com/2019/10/24/entertainment/hocus-pocus-new-trnd/index.html" target="_blank">reportedly one in the works</a> over at Disney+.</p><img class="media__image media__image--responsive" alt="The trio appear in a scene from the 1993 film &amp;quot;Hocus Pocus.&amp;quot;" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhEAAJAJEAAAAAAP///////wAAACH5BAEAAAIALAAAAAAQAAkAAAIKlI+py+0Po5yUFQA7"><p class="img__preloader"></p><img alt="The trio appear in a scene from the 1993 film &amp;quot;Hocus Pocus.&amp;quot;" class="media__image" src="//cdn.cnn.com/cnnnext/dam/assets/191024075903-hocus-pocus-movie-still-large-169.jpg"><p class="element-raw appearance-fullwidth">The trio appear in a scene from the 1993 film "Hocus Pocus."</p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">But the latest reunion wasn't for a new movie; it's for Midler's annual fundraiser known as Hulaween, benefiting her nonprofit, the New York Restoration Project, which works to restore gardens and parks in New York's five boroughs.</p><p></p><ul class="cn cn-list-hierarchical-xs cn--idx-11 cn-zoneAdContainer"></ul><a></a><p>This year's event, "In Search of the Sanderson Sisters: A Hocus Pocus Hulaween Takeover," is virtual due to the <a href="https://www.cnn.com/world/live-news/coronavirus-pandemic-10-19-20-intl/index.html" target="_blank">Covid-19 pandemic</a> </p><p class="zn-body__paragraph">Although it's unclear what the stars have in store, they've clearly already put a spell on their fans.</p></div>
