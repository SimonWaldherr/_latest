<div><p id="kAJTkn">A light heavyweight matchup is being targeted for December.</p>
<p id="XdJEkc">Multiple people with knowledge of the promotion&#8217;s plans confirmed to MMA Fighting that a bout between <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/313/ovince-st-preux">Ovince Saint Preux</a> and <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/5050/jamahal-hill">Jamahal Hill</a> has been added to the UFC&#8217;s event on Dec. 5. The fight card is scheduled to take place at the UFC APEX in Las Vegas.</p>
<p id="KXOAKc"><a href="https://cagesidepress.com/2020/10/21/ufc-ovince-saint-preux-vs-jamahal-hill-targeted-december-5/">Cageside Press</a> was the first to report the booking. </p>
<p id="Xjq51x">Saint Preux will look to make it three straight victories at 205-pounds. &#8220;OSP&#8221; submitted Micha&#322; Oleksiejczuk at UFC Copenhagen in September 2019 and made his 2020 debut in the heavyweight division, dropping a split decision to <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/492/ben-rothwell">Ben Rothwell</a> in May. The former interim title challenger returned to his home at 205 at September&#8217;s UFC Vegas 9 event and knocked out <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/3432/alonzo-menifield">Alonzo Menifield</a> in the second round. </p>
<p id="cvsS3n">On Wednesday, the 37-year-old revealed via Twitter that he signed a new contract with the UFC. </p>

<p id="lgbtFZ">Hill has won all eight of his professional bouts, including both of his octagon appearances. After earning a contract on <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/2230/dana-white">Dana White</a>&#8217;s Contender Series in July 2019, &#8220;Sweet Dreams&#8221; earned a unanimous decision over <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/4436/darko-stosic">Darko Stosic</a> in January at UFC Raleigh. In his most recent bout, Hill opened up a lot of eyeballs with a devastating finish of <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/4812/klidson-abreu">Klidson Abreu</a> in under two minutes at UFC on ESPN 9 in May. </p>
<p id="flSbSR">The UFC&#8217;s Dec. 5 event will be headlined by a pivotal middleweight showdown between <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/1583/jack-hermansson">Jack Hermansson</a> and <a href="https://www.mmafighting.com/fighter/3278/darren-till">Darren Till</a>. </p>
