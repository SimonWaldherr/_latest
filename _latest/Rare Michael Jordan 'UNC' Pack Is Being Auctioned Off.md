<div><p><a target="_blank" href="http://solecollector.com/tag/sothebys">Sotheby's</a> launched&#160;its own <a target="_blank" href="https://solecollector.com/news/2020/10/sothebys-the-sneakers-shop">sneaker store</a> earlier this month, but that doesn't mean it will stop&#160;auctioning off rare sneakers.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>The latest from the world-renowned auction house is this&#160;<a target="_blank" href="https://solecollector.com/news/2012/04/jordan-x-converse-30th-anniversary-pro-leather-auctions-officially-launched">Jordan Brand&#160;x Converse "UNC"&#160;pack,</a>&#160;which was created&#160;in 2012 to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the&#160;University of North Carolina's&#160;National Championship&#160;win where&#160;Michael Jordan hit the game-winning shot to give&#160;the Tar Heels the&#160;victory.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<a target="_blank" href="https://images.solecollector.com/images/fl_lossy,q_auto/t5hfhy6ciio5wrjwxthl/jordan-brand-x-converse-unc-pack-2012-sothebys-auction"><img alt="Jordan Brand x Converse 'UNC' Pack 2012 Sotheby's Auction" src="https://images.solecollector.com/images/fl_lossy,q_auto/t5hfhy6ciio5wrjwxthl/jordan-brand-x-converse-unc-pack-2012-sothebys-auction"></a>&#13;
Image via Sotheby's&#13;
&#13;
&#13;
<p>The lot consists of the <a target="_blank" href="https://solecollector.com/sd/00008/jordan/">Jordan</a> x <a target="_blank" href="https://solecollector.com/sd/00831/converse/converse-pro-leather">Converse Pro Leather</a> shoe (size 13) as well as two of MJ's&#160;jerseys featuring the UNC colors&#160;including one that he wore&#160;in practice and the other bearing&#160;his autograph on the back.&#160;Only 30 units of the pack were produced&#160;and this specific unit number 9 was provided by the&#160;James R. Jordan Foundation, which was founded&#160;by MJ's mother, Deloris, in honor of her late husband.</p>&#13;
&#13;
<p>Bidding for this&#160;Jordan Brand&#160;x Converse "UNC"&#160;pack will run from Nov. 2 to Nov. 10&#160;at <a target="_blank" href="https://www.sothebys.com/en/buy/auction/2020/james-r-jordan-foundation-capsule-collection/michael-jordan-converse-commemorative-pack">Sothebys.com</a>. The auction is&#160;estimated to fetch anywhere between&#160;$100,000&#160;to $110,000. All of the proceeds will benefit the&#160;James R. Jordan Foundation, a non-profit organization&#160;that provides&#160;families in underserved communities&#160;access to educational resources.</p>&#13;
