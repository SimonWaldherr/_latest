<div><p>ABU DHABI &#8211; You can watch today&#8217;s press conference from Yas Beach in advance of UFC 254 here on MMAjunkie at 9 a.m. ET (5 p.m. GST local time).</p>
<p>UFC president Dana White hosts the event, which features UFC lightweight champion <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/khabib-nurmagomedov">Khabib Nurmagomedov</a> (28-0 MMA, 12-0 UFC) and interim lightweight titleholder <a href="https://mmajunkie.usatoday.com/tag/justin-gaethje">Justin Gaethje</a> (22-2 MMA, 5-2 UFC), who square off in the main event of Saturday&#8217;s event at the nearby Flash Forum.</p>
<p>The UFC 254 main card airs on pay-per-view at a special time of 2 p.m. ET following prelims on ESPN2/ESPN+ and early prelims on UFC Fight Pass/ESPN+.</p>
<p>The press conference streams live, courtesy of the UFC, and the two fighters are expected to faceoff after answering questions from the media. It&#8217;s the last time Nurmagomedov and Gaethje are scheduled to appear together before Friday&#8217;s official weigh-ins and ceremonial faceoffs.</p>
<p>MMA Junkie will have full coverage of the full week&#8217;s events from Yas Island.</p>
