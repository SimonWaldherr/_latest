<div><p id="Hb7wO7">The PGA Tour is headed to Sherwood Country Club in Southern California this weekend for the ZOZO CHAMPIONSHIP at Sherwood. The tournament features a 78-golfer field and will feature Tiger Woods in his second appearance of the 2020-21 season.</p>
<p id="cFb1Cj">Jon Rahm has been installed as the favorite at <a href="https://sportsbook.draftkings.com/leagues/golf/1715?category=tournament-lines&amp;subcategory=finishing-position">DraftKings Sportsbook</a>, listed at +1000. The rest of the top five includes Xander Schauffele at +1100, Rory McIlroy and Justin Thomas at +1200, and Tyrrell Hatton and Webb Simpson at +1800. Woods is listed at +3300.</p>
<p id="J5dfb1">Woods played in the rescheduled <a href="http://www.sbnation.com/us-open-tennis">US Open</a> back in September, missing the cut with a +10. This week he plays in a tournament he won last year, albeit in a different location. Last year&#8217;s ZOZO CHAMPIONSHIP took place at Narashino Country Club just outside Tokyo. This year&#8217;s tournament is in California due to the issues surrounding Covid-19.</p>
<p id="XnK8is">This is notable because Woods has had some success at this course. From 2000 to 2013, he hosted his Hero World Challenge at this course. Woods won five times, and while plenty has changed, familiarity with the course could be of some value for him.</p>
