<div><p>ONE Championship is helping turn October pink for Breast Cancer Awareness Month.</p>
<p>Breast Cancer Awareness Month is an international campaign that seeks to educate and raise funds to combat the disease.</p>
<p>The Home Of Martial Arts will be giving customers a free We Are ONE tote bag with every order and donating 20 percent of proceeds for sales of select <a href="https://one.shop/collections/breast-cancer-awareness">breast cancer awareness merchandise</a> exclusively available at <a href="https://one.shop/">ONE.SHOP</a>.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-237979" src="https://cdn.onefc.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/BCAM-Square-5.jpg" alt="ONE.SHOP Breast Cancer Awareness Month tote bag" width="1200" height="1200"></p>
<p>The donations will be made to the <a href="https://www.bcrf.org/">Breast Cancer Research Foundation</a> (BCRF), the <a href="https://www.bcf.org.sg/">Breast Cancer Foundation</a> (BCF), and the <a href="https://www.icanservefoundation.org/">ICanServe Foundation</a>.</p>
<p>The BCRF, founded in 1993, is a nonprofit organization committed to achieving prevention and a cure for breast cancer. The BCRF is the highest-rated breast cancer charity in the United States and has raised over a half-billion dollars for research.</p>
<p>The BCF, which was founded in 1997, is a social service agency in Singapore that has taken on the mission of eradicating breast cancer as a life-threatening disease.</p>
<p>Lastly, the ICanServe Foundation was founded in the Philippines in 1999, and the nonprofit organization provides hope and help to women who have been diagnosed with breast cancer.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-237978" src="https://cdn.onefc.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/BCAM-Square-2.jpg" alt="ONE.SHOP Breast Cancer Awareness Month hoodies" width="1200" height="1200"></p>
<p>Among the select items at ONE.SHOP are a We Are ONE t-shirt, hoodie, and crop top.</p>
<p>The hoodie, available in both <a href="https://one.shop/collections/breast-cancer-awareness/products/b-c-a-m-we-are-one-hoodie-black">black</a> and <a href="https://one.shop/collections/breast-cancer-awareness/products/b-c-a-m-we-are-one-hoodie-pink">pink</a>, is a 100 percent cotton pullover with pockets. The center design features the ONE logo with a rose and has the phrase &#8220;We Are ONE&#8221; printed on the sleeves. As fall and winter set in, the hoodie will give you both style and warmth.</p>
<p>The We Are ONE <a href="https://one.shop/collections/breast-cancer-awareness/products/b-c-a-m-we-are-one-tee-black">t-shirt</a> and <a href="https://one.shop/collections/breast-cancer-awareness/products/b-c-a-m-we-are-one-crop-top-black">crop tops</a> both feature screen-printed art on the front with the ONE logo printed on the sleeve.</p>
<p>The <a href="https://one.shop/collections/breast-cancer-awareness/products/b-c-a-m-we-are-one-tote-bag-black">tote bag</a>, free with every Breast Cancer Awareness We Are One collection purchase &#8211; or for US$15 separately &#8211; features the same screen-printed design as the t-shirt.</p>
