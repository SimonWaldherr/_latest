Cuomo on Sunday repeated calls for Orthodox Jewish communities to stop holding large gatherings. He quoted a Hebrew phrase and a line from the Hebrew Bible to make his case, a change in tone from comments last week that incensed Jewish leaders. He also took another dig at New York City, saying its handling of the clusters leads people to "dismiss the law." (Luiz C. Ribeiro/for New York Daily News)
