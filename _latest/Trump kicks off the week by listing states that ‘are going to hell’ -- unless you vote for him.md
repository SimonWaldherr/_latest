According to Real Clear Politics, Trump also trails Biden by a huge margin in New York. An average of major surveys show Biden leading the incumbent by 28.7% in the Empire State. The president is especially unpopular in New York City, where his name has been taken off buildings and a Black Lives Matter mural was painted in front of Trump Tower. In 2016, Hillary Clinton got 79% of the vote in the city, where Trump, who was raised in Queens, got only 19% of all ballots.
